Subject Two Hundred Ten

WHO WILL BE IN THE NEW JERUSALEM

Scripture: Rev. 21:27

  1. Who will be in the New Jerusalem?
    1. Those who repent and believe in Jesus.
    2. Those who receive the Lord Jesus as their Savior.
    3. Those whose sins are forgiven.
    4. Those who have obtained God’s life.
    5. Those whose names are written in the book of life.
    6. Those who follow the Lamb.
  2. How to enter into the New Jerusalem
    Develop this point.
