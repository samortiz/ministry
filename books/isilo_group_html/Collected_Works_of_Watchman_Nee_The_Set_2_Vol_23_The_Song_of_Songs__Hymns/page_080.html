HYMN NO. 61

8.5.8.5.D.

Hosts of darkness are approaching
    Satan leading on;
Lo, some fellow troops retreating,
    Courage almost gone.

(Chorus)
    Ho, be brave with faith unbending,
        Standing patiently;
    Soon by grace we'll shame the devil,
        Gain the victory.

Lo, the blood-stained banner waving,
    Hark, the trumpet sound;
In the pow'r of Christ we're standing,
    Triumph will abound.

Jesus tells us, "I come quickly,
    Stand, be watchful still."
We declare to highest heavens,
    "By Your grace we will."

 

HYMN NO. 62

6.6.8.6.

    God's people have been robbed,
    And will not God avenge?
The Lord has said He surely will;
    We needn't fear nor cringe.

    We cry out night and day;
    Will God not lend His ear?
Assuredly God's move is fast,
    Vengeance from heaven's near.

    When Satan presses hard,
    Can God not reprehend?
Assuredly, God can and will,
    And Satan's mischief end.

    The enemy is fierce,
    Our Lord has promised this.
We should cry out continually,
    The termination his.

    So we must not lose heart,
    But ever stronger be.
Appeal to God both day and night;
    Impair the enemy.
