Introduction to Malachi:
Author: Malachi (1:1).Time of His Ministry: About 430 B.C., at Nehemiah's time.Place of His Ministry: Jerusalem.Object of His Ministry: The Israelites who had returned from captivity.

Subject of Malachi:
Jehovah's Dealing with the Sons of Levi (the Priests among Israel)
and with the Sons of Jacob (the People of Israel)
