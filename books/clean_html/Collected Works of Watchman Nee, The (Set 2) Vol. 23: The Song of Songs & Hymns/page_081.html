HYMN NO. 63

7.6.7.6.D.

God's servants must now proclaim
    The Cross's message high;
The gospel fully preached will
    Our Lord now satisfy.
The vict'ry on Golgotha
    We all should testify;
Before the King victorious
    All hell will quake and cry.

Proclaim the Lord's salvation,
    Not for one hour restrained;
This message is so urgent,
    When preached, much will be gained.
His death, th'answer to our sins,
    Inspires us now to lead;
When He marched to Golgotha,
    We too received His deed.

God's servants must proclaim it,
    In every corner know:
Our Lord's work on Golgotha
    Has leveled Satan's throne.
Our Lord said, "It's finished."
    How wonderful the word!
Shame Satan with this message
    Where it has not been heard.

Divine good news, proclaim it,
    Nor for our failures fret;
We sit with Christ in glory,
    The pow'rs of hell upset.
Though fierce in time with battle,
    Christ's banner widely fling;
He's stronger than the strong one,
    He is the conqu'ring King.

God's servants must be preaching
    To trumpet certain sound;
The place where we are standing
    Is always vict'ry ground.
Oh, let us sing, proclaiming
    What this poor world should see:
God's Christ, our King of glory
    Has tamed the enemy.

God's servant must stand fearless;
    Bold courage now display.
Our king is coming quickly,
    Maybe it is today.
Though Satan's rumors trouble,
    The Word must grow and gain.
In war Christ's grace is greater,
    Greater reward to gain.
