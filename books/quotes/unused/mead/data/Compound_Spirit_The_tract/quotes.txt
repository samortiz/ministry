[1]  This does not destroy the distinction between God the Son and God the Spirit, but it does indicate that after Christ s resurrection the Spirit bears and communicates this glorious God-man to us.
[2]  Before Christ s resurrection, the Spirit of God possessed the divine nature only; but after Christ s resurrection, both His divinity and humanity have been compounded into the life-giving Spirit.
[3]  When we believe in Christ, the Spirit enters us, bearing all that Christ is to us, including the preciousness of His death, the effectiveness of His death, the preciousness of His resurrection, and the power of His resurrection.
