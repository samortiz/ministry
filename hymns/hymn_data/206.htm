Hymns, #206

Praise of the Lord - Satisfaction with Him

E206 C167 K167 S94 T206

Lyrics:Anne Ross Cousin
Music:Arr. from Chretien d'Urhan, 1834, arr. by Edward F. Rimbault
Meter:7.6.7.6.D

Hymns using same tune:

#169, #1323

Verses:  1   2   3 

 
 1 

O Christ, He is the fountain,

The deep, sweet well of life:

Its living streams I've tasted

Which save from grief and strife.

And to an ocean fullness,

His mercy doth expand;

His grace is all-sufficient

As by His wisdom planned.
 
 2 

O I am my Beloved's.

And my Beloved's mine;

He brings a poor vile sinner

Into His house of wine!

I stand upon His merit;

I know no other stand.

I'm hidden in His presence

And held by His own hand.
 
 3 

The Bride eyes not her garment,

But her dear Bridegroom's face;

I will not gaze at glory.

But on my King of grace:

Not at the crown He giveth,

But on His pierced hand;

The Lamb is all the glory,

And my eternal stand!
