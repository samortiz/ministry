[1]  In the Old Testament there is another particular designation of God as the Spirit, the Spirit of Jehovah.
[2]  Hence, Jehovah designates God in His personal relationship with His chosen people, and the Spirit of Jehovah designates God as the Spirit coming to man in that personal relationship.
[3]  The Holy Spirit is God the Spirit coming to man to apply God s holy, divine nature to man that man may be holy as God is.
