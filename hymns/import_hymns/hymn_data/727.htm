Comfort in Trials - By Gain From Suffering

Music:Ira D. Sankey
Meter:5.4.5.4.D

Verses:  1   2   3 

 
 1 

Light after darkness,

Gain after loss,

Strength after weakness,

Crown after cross;

Sweet after bitter,

Hope after fears,

Home after wand'ring,

Praise after tears.
 
 2 

Sheaves after sowing,

Sun after rain,

Sight after mystery,

Peace after pain;

Joy after sorrow,

Calm after blast,

Rest after weariness,

Sweet rest at last.
 
 3 

Near after distant,

Gleam after gloom,

Love after loneliness,

Life after tomb;

After long agony,

Rapture of bliss,

Right was the pathway,

Leading to this.
