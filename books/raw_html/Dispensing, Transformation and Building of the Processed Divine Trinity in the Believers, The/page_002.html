CHAPTER ONE

THE DISPENSING OF THE PROCESSED DIVINE TRINITY IN THE BELIEVERS

(1)

Scripture Reading: 1 Pet. 1:3; Titus 3:5; John 3:16; 2 Pet. 1:4b; 2 Cor. 5:17; Rom. 5:10; John 1:12-13; 1 Cor. 15:22; Rom. 12:5; 2 Cor. 4:16b; Rom. 12:2a; 8:2, 4-6

OUTLINE

  1. Through regeneration—1 Pet. 1:3; Titus 3:5b:
    1. God the Father dispenses His divine life and holy nature into the believers—John 3:16; 2 Pet. 1:4b.
    2. This is the beginning of the divine dispensing.
    3. That the fallen old creation in Adam may become the new creation in Christ—2 Cor. 5:17.
    4. That sinners as God’s enemies may become children of God—Rom. 5:10; John 1:12-13.
    5. That the dead in Adam may become the organic Body of Christ—1 Cor. 15:22; Rom. 12:5.
  2. Through renewing:
    1. Following the dispensing in regeneration to dispense God’s life element continuously into the believers’ spirit by the Holy Spirit—Titus 3:5c; 2 Cor. 4:16b.
    2. That the believers’ inward parts, beginning from their mind, may be renewed metabolically—Rom. 12:2a.
    3. The believers need to have their inward parts joined to the Spirit in order to absorb the divine element distributed by the Spirit—Rom. 8:5-6.
    4. The believers also need to walk according to the spirit that their inward parts may be renewed by being saturated with the Spirit until their entire being becomes a new creation that is fully organic—Rom. 8:4.
    5. The issue—the entire being of the believers is filled and saturated with the Spirit of life to be God’s spiritual new creation—Rom. 8:2.
