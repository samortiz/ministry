John 15:5, 4
I am the vine,
You are the branches;
He who abides in Me
And I in Him,
He bears much fruit,
For apart from Me, apart from Me
You can do nothing.
Abide in Me
And I in you.
For the branch cannot bear fruit of itself
Unless it abides
In the vine
So neither can you unless you abide in Me.
