HYMN NO. 91

8.6.8.6. with chorus.

His promises indelible,
    His words are firmly fixed.
Faith has no need of witnesses,
    From word to deed is fixed.

(Chorus)
    Heav'n and earth will be set afire,
    High mountains and hills will expire;
    Souls in faith in Him retire
        Knowing His word is sure.

Oh, with a heart like Abraham's
    Look up into the sky
And count the stars, not problems brief;
    Faith firm will testify.

There's dark clouds thick and threatening,
    But just above, it's bright;
By waiting just a few more hours,
    The morning brings pure light.

In face of persecution calm,
    E'en in the furnace flame;
Our work together with the Son
    Will cause the devil's shame.

Our friends may even forsake us,
    So we approach God's face;
Jesus Himself our power is,
    Provider of our grace.

In everything His power trust
    Until the rapture own.
The things that we believe in will
    One day be fully shown.

 

HYMN NO. 92

10.10.10.10.

Our home is higher than the highest stars;
There, there, our place, our final destiny.
We're looking upward from this land of scars;
There, there the Lord prepared a rest for me.

The New Jerusalem by God is built,
Which He will give to those redeemed by grace.
Those souls for whom His precious blood was spilt,
They all can enter then into that place.

Whenever I upon that city gaze,
Each time I long His glorious face to see.
I would be raptured, all my heart ablaze
To be with Him, my God, eternally.

How I wish always in His house to live,
Rejoicing with all saints, never to part,
Ever for endless love my praise to give,
Cherishing full salvation in my heart.

What I admire is not the street of gold,
Nor what I long for is some earthly good.
That I will be with Him, this thought I hold,
With Him who bought my soul with precious blood.

O Jesus, Lord, fulfill our heart's desire;
Bring us into the Father's blessed land.
To be with You, this thought our hearts inspire,
To praise You more, Your ways to understand.

Lord, by that time You will make all things new,
And You will also wipe away all tears;
All creatures will join in, Your praise pursue,
Your visage to enjoy through endless years.
