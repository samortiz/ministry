PREFACE

This book is a collection of messages given by Witness Lee in Hong Kong in October and November 1955. Consisting of twenty-one chapters, this book focuses on the matter of life as the central goal of God in the universe. It also speaks of how God can be life to man and how man can live an overcoming life unto the maturity in life. The messages have been translated from the original Chinese.
