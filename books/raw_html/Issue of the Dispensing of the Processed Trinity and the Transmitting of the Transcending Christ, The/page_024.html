CHAPTER FOUR

THE ISSUE OF THE TRANSMITTING
OF THE TRANSCENDING CHRIST

Scripture Reading: Eph. 1:15-23

Hymns: #885, #890

OUTLINE

  1. The apostle's prayer for the believers—vv. 15-20a:
    1. Thanking God for their faith in the Lord Jesus and their love to all the saints—vv. 15-16a.
    2. Asking God, the Father of glory—vv. 16b-20a:
      1. To give the believers a spirit of wisdom and revelation in the full knowledge of Christ—v. 17.
      2. To enlighten the eyes of their heart that they may know—v. 18a:
        1. What is the hope of God's calling—v. 18b.
        2. What are the riches of the glory of His inheritance in the saints—v. 18c.
        3. What is the surpassing greatness of God's power toward the believers, according to the operation of the might of God's strength, which God caused to operate in Christ —vv. 19-20a.
  2. Christ's transcending —vv. 20b-22b:
    1. Raised from the dead—v. 20b.
    2. Seated at God's right hand in the heavenlies—v. 20c:
      1. Far above all rule, authority, power, and lordship—v. 21a.
      2. Above every name that is named not only in this age but also in that which is to come —v. 21b.
    3. Having all things subjected under His feet —v. 22a.
    4. Given to be Head over all things—v. 22b.
  3. Christ's transmitting —vv. 22c-23:
    1. To the church, His Body—v. 23.
    2. Transmitting:
      1. His accomplishments in His incarnation, crucifixion, and resurrection.
      2. His victory in His resurrection.
      3. His attainments and obtainments in His ascension.
    3. Consummating in His heavenly ministry of:
      1. The Lord of all—Acts 2:36a.
      2. The Christ of God—Acts 2:36b.
      3. The Leader of all the rulers—Acts 5:31a.
      4. The Savior—Acts 5:31b.
      5. The High Priest—Heb. 4:15; 7:26.
      6. The Advocate—1 John 2:1b.
      7. The Intercessor—Heb. 7:25.
      8. The Mediator of the new covenant—Heb. 8:6.
      9. The surety of the new testament—Heb. 7:22.
      10. The Life-giver—John 10:10b.
      11. The Comforter—John 14:16-17.
      12. The Lamb-God—Rev. 22:1b.
