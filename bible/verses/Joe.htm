Introduction to Joel:
Author: Joel (1:1).Time of His Ministry: About 800 B.C., probably after the prophet Elisha.Place of His Ministry: The southern kingdom of Judah.Object of His Ministry: The southern kingdom of Judah.

Subject of Joel:
The Devastation of the Human Government on Israel in Four Stages
and Christ's Destruction of the Devastators and
His Reign among Israel in the Restoration
