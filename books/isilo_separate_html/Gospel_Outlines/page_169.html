Subject One Hundred Sixty-eight

CHRIST HAS SET US FREE

Scripture: Gal. 5:1

  1. Our bondage
    1. We are under the bondage of sin.
    2. We are under the bondage of the law.
    3. We are under the bondage of Satan.
    4. We are under the bondage of death.
  2. Christ’s setting free
    1. Christ dealt with sin for us.
    2. Christ satisfied the law for us.
    3. Christ destroyed Satan for us.
    4. Christ eliminated death for us.
  3. The purpose of salvation
    1. To forgive our sins and deliver us from sin.
    2. To deliver us from the law.
    3. To deliver us from Satan’s dominion.
    4. To deliver us from the power of death.
  4. How to be released
    Develop this point.
