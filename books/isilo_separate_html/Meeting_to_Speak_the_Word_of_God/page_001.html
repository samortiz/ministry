PREFACE

The messages contained in this book and in two related books entitled The Full Knowledge of the Word of God and Everyone Speaking the Word of God were given in October, 1985 in Taipei, Taiwan during a training in which the author spoke three times daily. These messages have been translated from the Chinese.
