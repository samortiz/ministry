PREFACE

This book is part of a three volume set containing messages given by Brother Witness Lee during the years 1978 and 1979.

This volume includes eleven messages given during weekly ministry meetings in Anaheim, California in the Fall of 1978.

The companion volumes, Life Messages, Volume One and Life Messages, Volume Two, are a collection of messages from conferences held in twenty-four cities throughout the United States and Southeast Asia.
