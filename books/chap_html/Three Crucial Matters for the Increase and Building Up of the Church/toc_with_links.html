PREFACE

This book is composed of messages on the practice of the God-ordained way given by Brother Witness Lee in Taipei between 1986 and 1989. Chapters 1 through 7 were given in1986, chapters 8 through 16 in 1987, chapters 17 and 18 in 1988, and chapters 19 through 22 were given in 1989 to the saints who were involved in the spread of the gospel in Taiwan. These messages were translated from the original Chinese.
