TRUTH LESSONS—LEVEL THREE
THE TYPES IN THE OLD TESTAMENT

LESSON SEVEN

THE RAM PROVIDED BY GOD

OUTLINE

  1. A ram (a male sheep):
    1. A male signifying Christ as the strong One.
    2. A sheep signifying Christ as the meek, gentle, and perfect One.
  2. Typifying Christ as the One provided by God:
    1. Leaving the freedom of His divinity.
    2. His fighting power being caught in His humanity.
  3. Typifying Christ as the Substitute of those who inherit God’s promised inheritance:
    1. Dying on their behalf to satisfy God’s requirement.
    2. Living on their behalf to satisfy God’s desire:
      1. That they may live to righteousness.
      2. That they may become the righteousness of God.
