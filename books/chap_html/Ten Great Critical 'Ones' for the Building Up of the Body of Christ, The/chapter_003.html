THE TEN GREAT CRITICAL “ONES” FOR THE BUILDING UP OF THE BODY OF CHRIST

MESSAGE THREE

ONE CHRIST—THE UNSEARCHABLE ONE

(2)

OUTLINE

  1. He is the Shepherd of God’s flock, the door for them to come out of Judaism, and the pasture for them to feed on—John 10:11, 14, 16, 9.
  2. He is the resurrection, the life, the way, and the reality—John 11:25; 14:6.
  3. He is the Bridegroom who has the bride—John 3:29a.
  4. He is the power of God and the wisdom to His believers from God: righteousness, sanctification, and redemption—1 Cor. 1:24, 30.
  5. He is the Passover for the believers to pass through God’s judgment—1 Cor. 5:7.
  6. He is the spiritual food and the spiritual rock flowing with the living water for God’s people—1 Cor. 10:3-4; John 6:35; 4:10, 14.
  7. He is the Body (1 Cor. 12:12) and all the members of the Body—the new man (Col. 3:10-11).
  8. He is the firstfruits, the second man, and the last Adam—1 Cor. 15:20, 23, 47, 45.
  9. He is the Firstborn from the dead to be the Head of the Body—Col. 1:18.
  10. He is God’s allotted portion to the saints—Col. 1:12.
  11. He is the body (the reality) of all the positive things to God’s people—Col. 2:16-17.
  12. He is the life of His believers and the One whom they live and magnify—Col. 3:4; Phil. 1:20-21.
  13. He is the Lamb accomplishing God’s redemption and the Lion fighting for God’s kingdom—Rev. 5:5-6.
  14. He is the opener of the seals of the secrets of this age—Rev. 5:5.
  15. He is His believers’ hope of glory—Col. 1:27.
  16. He is the morning star who will appear to His overcomers before His coming back to the earth—Rev. 2:28.
  17. He is the Sun of righteousness with healing in His wings to appear to the world—Mal. 4:2.
  18. His riches dispensed into His saints are unsearchable—Eph. 3:8.
  19. He is the Savior coming back to:
    1. Transfigure His believers—Phil. 3:21.
    2. Judge His believers to discern who among them should be rewarded with the kingdom and its enjoyment as a reward for one thousand years; and who among them should be disciplined in the kingdom age, thus missing the kingdom as a reward—2 Cor. 5:10.
    3. Marry His overcomers as His bride in the millennial age—Rev. 19:7.
    4. Defeat Antichrist and his armies—Rev. 19:19-21.
    5. Be received by the remnant of the Jews—Zech. 12:10; Rom. 11:26.
    6. Judge the nations—the living people (Acts 10:42)—on His throne of glory—Matt. 25:31.
  20. He will be the King to rule over the earth with His overcomers—Rev. 20:4, 6.
  21. He will judge all the dead people (Acts 10:42) at His great white throne—Rev. 20:11-12.
  22. In the New Jerusalem (Rev. 21:1—22:5) He will be:
    1. The Husband of God’s redeemed people as His wife in eternity—21:2.
    2. The temple with God—21:22.
    3. The lamp with God as the light in Him—21:23.
    4. The Lamb-God on the throne to rule over the new heaven and new earth—22:1.
    5. The tree of life nourishing the entire New Jerusalem—22:2.
