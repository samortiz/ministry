THE TRAINING AND THE PRACTICE OF THE VITAL GROUPS

MESSAGE ELEVEN

ABIDING IN THE LORD AND WALKING ACCORDING TO THE SPIRIT TO FLOW RIVERS OF LIVING WATER

Scripture Reading: John 15:5; 7:38-39; Rom. 8:4b; Acts 4:31; 1 Thes. 5:17-19

OUTLINE

  1. Abiding in the Lord-John 15:5.
  2. Walking according to the spirit (or, the Spirit)-Rom. 8:4b.
  3. To flow rivers of living water-John 7:38-39.
  4. By the fullness of the Spirit-Acts 4:31.
  5. Not quenching the Spirit by praying unceasingly and giving thanks in everything-1 Thes. 5:17-19.
