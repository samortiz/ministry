He goes before me,

And is beside me,

So I'm not afraid.

His hands uphold me,

His love enfolds me,

His truth it shall be

My shield and buckler,

So I'm not afraid.

His cross before me,

His banner o'er me,

So I'm not afraid.

We're pressing forward,

We're marching upward,

To Him unafraid.
