Subject Two Hundred Seven

THE TORMENT OF HELL

Scripture: Rev. 14:9-11; 21:8; Mark 9:43, 48

  1. Hell is the portion of God’s wrath
    (In Rev. 14:10 cup means portion.)
    1. Because of God’s love toward man, He accomplished redemption for him.
    2. If a man refuses to repent, he remains under God’s wrath.
    3. The totality of God’s wrath is hell.
  2. There is fire and brimstone in hell
    1. Fire burns man.
    2. Brimstone chokes man.
    3. The lake of fire which burns with brimstone is unquenchable.
  3. There are worms that do not die in hell
    To be bitten by mosquitoes, bugs, or fleas is already unbearable, but imagine the torment from the worms in hell which do not die with the unquenchable fire!
  4. How to escape hell
    Develop this point.
