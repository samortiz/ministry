Introduction to Micah:
Author: Micah (1:1).Time of His Ministry: About 750-710 B.C., at the time of Isaiah and Hosea.Place of His Ministry: The southern kingdom of Judah.Object of His Ministry: The whole nation of Israel, including Judah.

Subject of Micah:
Jehovah's Reproof on Israel and His Restoration of Israel
