Subject One Hundred Twenty-nine

EVERYONE WHO BELIEVES IN HIM
SHALL RECEIVE FORGIVENESS OF SINS

Scripture: Acts 10:43

  1. All are sinners
    1. Man has sinful deeds.
    2. Man is under condemnation.
  2. Jesus is the Savior
    1. Jesus is God incarnated to be the Savior of man. He died on the cross for our sins.
    2. He resurrected from the dead and His resurrection became the basis of our faith.
  3. Believe in Jesus
    1. To believe in Jesus is to believe that He is the Son of God.
    2. Believe that He resurrected from the dead.
    3. Believe all the words of the Scripture.
    4. In spirit receive Him as your Savior.
  4. The result of believing in the Lord
    1. Your sins are forgiven.
    2. The assurance of the forgiveness of sins is found in this verse: “Everyone who believes in Him shall receive forgiveness of sins.”
