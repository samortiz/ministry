PREFACE

The Crucial Words of Leading in the Lord’s Recovery series contains messages released by Brother Witness Lee between 1984 and 1989 in Taiwan while leading the churches to practice the God-ordained way. Books 1 and 2 contain messages released during two times of training for elders and co-workers. The remaining messages, beginning in Book 3, are compiled according to chronological order. This book is composed of messages released in 1984.
