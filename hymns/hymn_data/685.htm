Comfort in Trials - By the Lord's Presence

E685 C- K- S- T-

Lyrics:Unknown
Music:A. Legge
Meter:5.4.5.4.D

This hymn has a unique tune

Verses:  1   2   3 

 
 1 

Lord, Thou art with me!

Whom shall I fear?

Who then can harm me

While Thou art near?

Under Thy shadow

Is my retreat;

There to my taste,

Lord, Thy fruit is sweet.
 
 2 

Lord, Thou art with me,

And Thou dost give

Strength for the journey,

Grace now to live.

Mercies each morning

Thou dost renew;

And for my prospect,

Thy rest in view.
 
 3 

Lord, Thou art with me;

Onward I move;

Each day I'm proving

More of Thy love.

Now Thou art training,

Setting me free

From all that hinders,

To dwell with Thee.
