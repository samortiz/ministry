This book is a translation of messages given by Brother Witness Lee in the Chinese New Year’s Conference in Anaheim, California in February 1984; in the Young People’s Conference in Anaheim in August 1984; and in a Chinese-speaking working saints’ meeting in Alhambra, California in April 1991. These messages were not reviewed by the speaker.
