LESSON FOURTEEN

A KEY VIEW OF THE OLD TESTAMENT

(8)

THE OLD TESTAMENT PROPHETS

OUTLINE

  1. The function of the prophets.
  2. The prophets before the age of the prophets:
    1. Abraham.
    2. Moses.
    3. Samuel.
    4. David.
    5. The Gentile prophet Balaam.
  3. The prophets during the age of the prophets:
    1. The raising up of the prophethood.
    2. The prophets who did not leave any prophetic writings:
      1. Gad.
      2. Nathan.
      3. The old prophet.
      4. Ahijah.
      5. Elijah.
      6. Elisha.
      7. Micaiah.
    3. The prophets who wrote the books of the Major Prophets:
      1. Isaiah.
      2. Jeremiah.
      3. Ezekiel.
      4. Daniel.
    4. The prophets who wrote the books of the Minor Prophets:
      1. Hosea.
      2. Joel.
      3. Amos.
      4. Obadiah.
      5. Jonah.
      6. Micah.
      7. Nahum.
      8. Habakkuk.
      9. Zephaniah.
      10. Haggai.
      11. Zechariah.
      12. Malachi.
