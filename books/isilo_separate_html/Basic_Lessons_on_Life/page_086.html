LESSON EIGHTEEN

THE EXERCISE OF OUR SPIRIT
FOR GODLINESS

Scripture Reading: 1 Tim. 1:3-4; 3:15-16; 4:7-8; 6:11; 2 Tim. 3:12, 5; 4:22; 2:22

OUTLINE

  1. God's economy—1 Tim. 1:3-4.
  2. The mystery of godliness—1 Tim. 3:15-16:
    1. God manifested in the flesh.
    2. The church of the living God.
  3. Exercising ourselves unto godliness—1 Tim. 4:7-8:
    1. Bodily exercise profiting for a little.
    2. Godliness being profitable for all things:
      1. Of the present life.
      2. Of the coming life.
    3. Pursuing godliness to be a man of God—1 Tim. 6:11.
    4. Living godly in Christ—2 Tim. 3:12.
    5. Having the power of godliness—2 Tim. 3:5.
    6. The Lord being with our spirit—2 Tim. 4:22:
      1. Contacting the Lord by exercising our spirit.
      2. Thus receiving grace.
    7. Calling on the Lord—2 Tim. 2:22:
      1. Out of a pure heart.
      2. With the Lord's callers.
