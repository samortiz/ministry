HYMN NO. 24

11.11.11.11.

Standing on the victory of Christ my Lord,
Standing on the power of the Son of God,
Standing on the blood of His New Testament,
Standing on the words that show His faithfulness.

 

HYMN NO. 25

8.6.8.6.

O grave, your power is annulled;
    The sting of death destroyed.
Released from all these dark controls,
    All day I sing o'erjoyed.
His grace cannot be done away,
    Eternal life is mine;
I know that I will never die,
    Made free by life divine.

 

HYMN NO. 26

8.7.8.7.D.

Heav'nly glory, heav'nly brightness,
    Universal King descends;
Saints are raptured, Hades finished,
    Songs are sung in unison.
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
    Son of God so splendorous.
Thine the vict'ry, thine the honor!
    Grace to all so glorious.

 

HYMN NO. 27

7.7.7.7.

In the Lord's victorious name,
    Overcomers we must be;
Stand and shame the evil one;
    Fighting is our destiny.

Jesus' name, our battle cry,
    He the power in our breast,
Son of man, who routs the foe,
    Leading man to joy and rest.

Fight the devil to retreat,
    Smash his ev'ry stronghold here;
Take full courage, slumber not:
    Christ will call His soldiers dear.

In the Lord's victorious name,
    Overcomers we must be;
Stand and shame the evil one;
    Fighting is our destiny.
