[1]  We will speak a little on the way to worship God.
[2]  Others think that because they have not performed well and have done many things that displease God, they cannot come to God to worship Him.
[3]  If you think that the blood of the Lord Jesus is not enough for you to draw near to Him, I will tell you honestly that you will never be able to come to Him or worship Him.
[4]  Every believer can worship within the Holy of Holies, and no one can worship in place of another.
[5]  The way they would come and worship before God is the same way we come and worship before God.
