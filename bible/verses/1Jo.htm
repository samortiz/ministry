Introduction to 1 John:
Author: John, the author of the Gospel of John, as indicated by this book's comparable style and tone.Time of Writing: Approximately A.D. 90-95, after John's return from exile on Patmos, according to church history and the content of this book.Place of Writing: Ephesus, where, according to church history, John ministered in his last days.Recipients: The believers in general, who have the eternal life of God by believing into the Son of God (5:11-13).
