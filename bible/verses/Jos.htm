Introduction to Joshua:
Author: Joshua (24:26).Time of Writing: 1452-1426 B.C. (see below).Place of the Record: The plains of Moab (cf. Deut. 34:8-9) and Shechem in Canaan (24:1, 25-26).Time Period Covered: About twenty-seven years, 1452-1426 B.C., from the first month of the forty-first year after the exodus (4:19) to the death of Joshua (24:29).
