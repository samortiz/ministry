Lesson Seven

THE TWO ASPECTS OF THE CHURCH- UNIVERSAL AND LOCAL

Scripture Reading

Matt. 16:16-18; Eph. 1:22-23; Matt. 18:17; Eph. 2:22; 4:4; Titus 1:5; Acts 14:23; 13:1; Rev. 1:4a, 11; Acts 2:42; 1 Cor. 10:16-17

Outline

  1. The truth according to the Bible
  2. The universal aspect
    1. Including all New Testament believers
    2. As revealed by the Lord in Matthew 16:18
  3. The local aspect
    1. The believers living in the local churches
    2. The local churches being the local expressions of the Body of Christ
    3. All the local churches constituting the one Body of Christ
  4. The genuine ground
    1. The ground of locality-one city, one church
    2. The ground of the genuine oneness-one body universally
  5. The local administration and the universal fellowship
    1. The administration being separate and equal locally
    2. The fellowship being one universally
