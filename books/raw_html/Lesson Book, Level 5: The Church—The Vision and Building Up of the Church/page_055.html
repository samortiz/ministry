Lesson Ten

THE FAILURES IN THE CHURCHES, THE DEGRADATION AND RECOVERY OF THE CHURCH

Scripture Reading

Acts 5:1-11; 6:1; 15:1-2; Col. 2:8, 16, 20-22; 2 Tim. 1:15; Ezra 1:3-11; 1 Tim. 1:3-4; John 5:17

Outline

  1. The failures in the churches
  2. The degradation of the church
    1. During the New Testament
    2. After the New Testament
  3. The recovery of the church
    1. Typified by the return of the children of Israel from their captivity
    2. Indicated in the later epistles of the New Testament
    3. To be recovered:
      1. From the divisive and apostate ground with its deviation from the truths concerning:
        1. The person of the Triune God
        2. The person and work of Christ
      2. Back to the unique and pure ground of the oneness of the Body of Christ with its truths concerning:
        1. The New Testament faith and Godâ€™s economy
        2. The person and work of Christ
        3. The person and the dispensing of the Triune God
        4. The church, the Body of Christ, the Corporate Christ
        5. The universal and local aspects of the church
      3. To build the Body of Christ, the temple of God, the house of God
      4. To establish the kingdom life
