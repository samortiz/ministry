TRUTH LESSONS—LEVEL ONE

LESSON NINETEEN

A KEY VIEW OF THE NEW TESTAMENT

(3)

JESUS CHRIST

(2)

FROM HIS RESURRECTION
TO HIS ETERNITY FUTURE

OUTLINE

  1. His resurrection:
    1. The process of His resurrection.
    2. The achievements of His resurrection.
    3. His breathing into the disciples in resurrection.
    4. His presence with the disciples in resurrection.
  2. His ascension:
    1. Ascending above all the heavens.
    2. To fill all things.
    3. Sitting down on the right hand of the Majesty on high.
    4. Enthroned and crowned.
    5. His offices and ministry in heaven:
      1. As Lord and Christ.
      2. As Head over all things.
      3. As the Baptizer in the Holy Spirit.
      4. As the Giver of spiritual gifts.
      5. As a Leader and Savior.
      6. As the Mediator and Surety of the better covenant.
      7. As a Minister of the true tabernacle in the heavens.
      8. As the High Priest.
      9. As an Advocate.
      10. As the Ruler of the kings of the earth.
      11. As the Opener of the mystery of the new covenant.
    6. The length of His stay in heaven.
  3. His second coming:
    1. First coming secretly from heaven to the air.
    2. Then coming openly from the air to the earth.
    3. Dealing with the four groups of people on the earth today.
    4. Binding and imprisoning Satan.
    5. Setting up the millennial kingdom on the earth.
    6. Purging the last rebellion of mankind.
    7. Judging all the dead in the universe.
  4. His eternity future:
    1. Being the entrance into the New Jerusalem.
    2. Being the Husband of the New Jerusalem.
    3. Being the temple of the holy city.
    4. Being the lamp of the holy city.
    5. Being the center of the holy city.
    6. Being the supply of the holy city.
  5. Conclusion.
