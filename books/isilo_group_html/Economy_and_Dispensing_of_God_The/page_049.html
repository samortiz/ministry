Making Us the Living Members of Christ,
the Organic Constituents of the Church,
as the Masterpiece, a Poem, of God,
through the Dispensing of the Divine Grace,
Which is the Triune God as a Gift Given to Us

It is through the dispensing of the divine grace, which is the Triune God as a gift given to us, that we become the living members of the Body of Christ and are made the organic constituents of the church as the divine and heavenly poem (Eph. 2:7-10). Such a change in the believers does not come about through the old way of preaching, but through the divine dispensing of the living Christ into them.

In Building Up the Church,
Which is the New Man and the Body,
as God's Dwelling Place in Our Spirit

1. By Creating, through Christ's Redemption
on the Cross, the Jewish and Gentile Believers
into One New Man in Himself as the Creating Life
and Element Dispensed into His Redeemed
out from His Divine Riches

2. By Reconciling Both
the Jewish and Gentile Believers
to God through the Cross in One Body,
Which Is Constituted with the Divine Life
and Element Released through the Death
of Christ and Dispensed into the
Believers in the Resurrection of Christ,
through Whom (the Son) We Both
(the Jewish and Gentile Believers)
Have Access in One Spirit unto
the Father in the Divine Dispensing
of the Divine Trinity All the Way
