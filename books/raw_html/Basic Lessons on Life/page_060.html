LESSON THIRTEEN

THE DIVINE LIFE DISPENSED
INTO THE THREE PARTS OF MAN

Scripture Reading: Rom. 8:2a, 6, 10, 11, 9

OUTLINE

  1. The divine life—v. 2a:
    1. The Spirit of life.
    2. In Christ Jesus.
  2. Dispensed into our spirit—v. 10:
    1. Christ in you.
    2. The spirit being life.
  3. Dispensed into our mind—v. 6b:
    1. The mind set on the spirit.
    2. The mind being life.
  4. Dispensed into our body—v. 11:
    1. Through God's indwelling Spirit.
    2. To give life to our mortal body.
  5. The dispensing of the Triune God—vv. 9-10a:
    1. God.
    2. The Spirit of God.
    3. The Spirit of Christ.
    4. Christ.
