TRUTH LESSONS—LEVEL ONE

LESSON EIGHTEEN

A KEY VIEW OF THE NEW TESTAMENT

(2)

JESUS CHRIST

(1)

FROM HIS ETERNITY PAST
TO HIS DEATH AND BURIAL

OUTLINE

  1. His eternity past:
    1. The Word that was in the beginning with God.
    2. The complete God.
    3. The only begotten Son of God.
    4. The expression of the Father.
    5. The Author of life.
  2. His status.
  3. His conception:
    1. Conceived of the Holy Spirit and born of a virgin.
    2. With God and man as His essences.
  4. His birth:
    1. In the reign of Caesar Augustus.
    2. At Bethlehem.
    3. Laid in a manger.
    4. Announced to the shepherds by an angel.
    5. A multitude of the heavenly army joining the angel and praising God.
    6. Witnessed by the shepherds and spoken of to others by them.
    7. Circumcised, named, and presented to God.
  5. His flight and growth.
  6. His human living.
  7. His four aspects.
  8. His baptism and test.
  9. His ministry:
    1. Preaching the gospel.
    2. Teaching people.
    3. Performing miracles and wonders:
      1. Calling things not being as being and ruling the universe.
      2. Casting out demons.
      3. Healing the sick.
      4. Cleansing the leper.
      5. Raising the dead.
    4. Expressing God.
  10. His death:
    1. The date and place of His death.
    2. The way of His death.
    3. Accomplishing an all-inclusive death with a sevenfold status.
    4. Accomplishing an eternal redemption:
      1. The Triune God dying for sinners in the human flesh of Jesus.
      2. Offering Himself to God through the eternal Spirit.
      3. The blood of Jesus the Son of God having an eternal efficacy.
  11. His burial.
