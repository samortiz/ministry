CHAPTER TWO

THE REGENERATED SPIRIT
OF THE BELIEVERS

Scripture Reading: Gen. 2:7; 2 Cor. 5:17; Eph. 3:16; 2:1, 5-6; John 3:5-6; 1 Pet. 1:3; John 3:36a; Gal. 2:20a; Eph. 2:22; 3:16b-17a; John 14:23b; 4:24; Rom. 1:9; 8:16; 2 Tim. 4:22; Eph. 1:17; 3:5; Rev. 1:10; 4:2; 17:3; 21:10; Eph. 4:23; 5:18; 1 Cor. 6:17

OUTLINE

  1. In God's old creation the spirit of man is the reality of man and not the person of man—Gen. 2:7.
  2. In God's new creation the spirit of the believers is the person of the man of the new creation (the inner man) of the believers—2 Cor. 5:17; Eph. 3:16:
    1. Being dead in sins—Eph. 2:1.
    2. Being revived and made alive through the resurrection of Christ—Eph. 2:5.
    3. And being enlivened and regenerated with the life of God by the life-giving Spirit in Christ's resurrection—Eph. 2:6; John 3:5-6; 1 Pet. 1:3.
  3. The regenerated spirit of the believers has:
    1. The life of God as the second life, the new life, of the believers—John 3:36a.
    2. Christ as the embodiment of the Triune God, the Father, Son, and Spirit, living in the believers to be the person of the believers as the new creation—Gal. 2:20a.
  4. The regenerated spirit of the believers becomes the dwelling place of the processed and consummated Triune God in them (Eph. 2:22), and it also becomes their inner man (Eph. 3:16b), in which Christ as the embodiment of the Triune God dwells (2 Tim. 4:22) and from which He spreads to make His home in their heart (Eph. 3:17a); this is the processed and consummated Triune God's making an abode in the believers (John 14:23b).
  5. The regenerated spirit of the believers becomes the organ for them to worship, serve, receive, possess, experience, and enjoy God—John 4:24; Rom. 1:9; 8:16; 2 Tim. 4:22.
  6. The regenerated spirit of the believers is also the organ for them to receive God's wisdom, revelation, and vision—Eph. 1:17; 3:5; Rev. 1:10; 4:2; 17:3; 21:10.
  7. The regenerated spirit of the believers spreads into their mind to become the renewing spirit in their mind—Eph. 4:23.
  8. The regenerated spirit of the believers is being filled with God—Eph. 5:18.
  9. The regenerated spirit of the believers and the consummated Spirit of God are mingled as one spirit—1 Cor. 6:17.
