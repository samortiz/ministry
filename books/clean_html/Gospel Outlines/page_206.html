Subject Two Hundred Five

THE ETERNAL JUDGMENT

Scripture: Rev. 20:11-15

  1. The conclusion of the last days
    1. The last days of the universe will be concluded by judgment.
    2. Of all God’s judgments the greatest will be His judgment upon men of all generations at the end of the present heavens and earth.
  2. God’s judgment
    1. God’s judgment at the end of the present heavens and earth will be executed at the great white throne. Whiteness signifies righteousness and purity. All of man’s unrighteousness and filthiness will be reflected in front of that throne of judgment.
    2. God’s judgment will be based upon the books before Him. These are the records of all of men’s words and deeds. God’s judgment will be made according to these records.
    3. God’s judgment in that day will be made according to what we do today.
  3. The result of God’s judgment
    1. All those whose names are not written in the book of life will be cast into the lake of fire.
    2. All those whose names are written in the book of life are the ones who have believed in the Lord Jesus and have obtained His life.
    3. The result of the judgment is that all those who did not believe in the Lord Jesus will be cast into the lake of fire.
    4. To be cast into the lake of fire is to perish forever.
  4. How to avoid the eternal judgment
    1. Repent and turn to God.
    2. Receive the Lord Jesus as our Savior. This is also to obtain God’s life so that our names may be written in the book of life. Only those whose names are written in the book of life can escape the eternal judgment.
