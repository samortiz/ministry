HYMN NO. 170

8.7.8.7. with chorus. (Hymns, #697)

Weary pilgrim on life's pathway—
    Struggling on beneath thy load—
Hear these words of consolation,
    "Cast thy burden on the Lord."

(Chorus)
    Cast thy burden on the Lord!
    Cast thy burden on the Lord,
    And He will strengthen thee,
        sustain and comfort thee—
    Cast thy burden on the Lord.

Are thy tired feet unsteady?
    Does thy lamp no light afford?
Is thy cross too great and heavy?
    "Cast thy burden on the Lord."

Are the ties of friendship severed?
    Hushed the voices fondly heard?
Breaks thy heart with weight of anguish?
    "Cast thy burden on the Lord."

Does thy heart with faintness falter?
    Does thy mind forget His word?
Does thy strength succumb to weakness?
    "Cast thy burden on the Lord."

He will hold thee up from falling;
    He will guide thy steps aright;
He will strengthen each endeavor;
    He will keep thee by His might.

 

HYMN NO. 171

8.7.8.7.D. with chorus.

Why grasp riches, harbor worry?
    God's own servant will be fed;
If our God controls your outlays
    You will not to drought be led.
Daily new demands may threaten,
    Daily He has fresh supply;
You are His endeared creation—
    Trust His path, not asking why.

(Chorus)
    Show your love and give your substance;
        Still some measure will remain.
    For your love and giving action,
        God will bless and bless again.

Trust! More time you needn't borrow;
    Though not fast, it is not slow.
Trust! The bottle will not empty;
    From His means He will bestow.
Though the lily may be naked,
    Though the bird is short of aid,
Yet the flour for the widow
    By her using didn't fade.

Why be anxious for tomorrow?
    In your hand is what He gave.
Wherefore, try tomorrow's worries?
    Thank your God for what you have.
Love is living, life is giving,
    Spending doesn't wealth reduce.
When your small supply seems wasted,
    Then God's blessing's more profuse.
