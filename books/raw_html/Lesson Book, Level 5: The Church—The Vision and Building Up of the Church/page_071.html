Lesson Twelve

THE CONSUMMATION OF THE CHURCH

Scripture Reading

Eph. 2:8; Rev. 5:9; Eph. 1:22-23; 2 Cor. 3:18; Rev. 12:5; 14:4; Matt. 25:21; Heb. 12:6; Rev. 21:14

Outline

  1. All the members of the church are produced in the church age
  2. The New Testament overcomers are also perfected in the church age
    1. The perfection of the New Testament overcomers in the church age
    2. The reward of the New Testament overcomers
      1. At the end of the church age
      2. In the age of the millennium
  3. The immature believers will be perfected in the age of the millennium
    1. Through the Lord’s dispensational and governmental discipline
    2. As illustrated by the case of the unfaithful slave
    3. As illustrated by the case of the slothful slave
  4. All the regenerated and perfected believers of the New Testament will be the components of the New Jerusalem, which is the church’s ultimate consummation
    1. Consummated in enjoying the eternal and divine life of the processed and consummated Triune God
    2. Consummated in experiencing the mingling of humanity with divinity
    3. Consummated in expressing the Triune God in His ultimate manifestation for eternity
