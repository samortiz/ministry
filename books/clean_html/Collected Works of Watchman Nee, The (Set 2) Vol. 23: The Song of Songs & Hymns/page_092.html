HYMN NO. 75

10.10.10.10.

We shall not die, but raptured see the Lord!
    This is the way our Father has made known.
Clearly the Holy Spirit gives the word;
    May we give up the world, turn to the throne.

We shall not die, but raptured see the Lord!
    How glorious! We'll know our heav'nly place!
In wink of time we'll quickly break the bonds
    Raptured, ascending, see Him face to face.

We shall not die, but raptured see the Lord!
    The truthful promise: He will soon appear.
Which day and hour not in our knowledge stored,
    Yet we can surely feel the time is near.

We shall not die; be wakeful and be clean.
    This age shall pass, soon heav'nly glories shine.
Letting no sin nor earthly homage stay,
    Forth from our life piety can now outshine.

We shall be raptured, meet Him in the air,
    And living now is not a wait to die;
So keep our spirit free from worldly fare,
    But look for His soon rapture from the sky.

We shall not die, but raptured see the Lord!
    Each day we're nearer, that sure dawning own.
Now persevere nor give up His reward,
    For soon our Lord will bring us to His throne.

We shall not die, but rapture soon ascend,
    God's children will enjoy the victory.
Come, Lord, do hear our spirit's pleading cries!
    Come so that with You we may ever be!
