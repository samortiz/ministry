TRUTH LESSONS—LEVEL ONE

LESSON TWENTY

A KEY VIEW OF THE NEW TESTAMENT

(4)

THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST

OUTLINE

  1. His person:
    1. The Creator.
    2. God—the Son of God.
    3. Man—the Son of Man.
    4. The Redeemer.
    5. The Savior.
    6. Jesus.
    7. Christ.
    8. The Lord.
    9. The resurrected Lord.
    10. The life-giving Spirit.
    11. The life.
    12. The resurrection.
  2. His accomplishments:
    1. Incarnation.
    2. Human experience.
    3. Death.
    4. Resurrection.
    5. Breathing the Spirit of life into the believers.
    6. Ascension.
    7. Pouring out the Spirit of power upon the believers, baptizing them into His Body.
  3. His attainments:
    1. The height of the universe.
    2. The peak of the divine attributes and human virtues.
  4. His obtainments:
    1. The throne.
    2. Glory.
    3. Honor.
    4. The name which is above every name.
    5. The lordship.
    6. The rulership.
  5. His work in the present age:
    1. Giving repentance and forgiveness of sins to save sinners.
    2. Building the church.
    3. Establishing God’s kingdom.
    4. Being the Mediator of the new covenant.
    5. Being the Minister of the true tabernacle.
    6. Being the High Priest of the believers.
    7. Being the Advocate of the believers.
  6. His work at His coming back:
    1. Redeeming the believers’ body.
    2. Restoring all things.
    3. Bringing in God’s kingdom.
  7. His work in eternity:
    1. Being the Lamb who is one with God.
    2. Being the Husband of the redeemed.
    3. Being the temple in which the redeemed serve God.
    4. Being the lamp of the holy city.
    5. Being the supply of the holy city.
    6. Being the healing of the nations on the new earth.
  8. Conclusion.
