THE TRIUNE GOD’S REVELATION AND HIS MOVE

MESSAGE ONE

THE TRIUNE GOD, HIS WORD, HIS REVELATION, HIS VISION, AND HIS MOVE

Scripture Reading: Matt. 28:19; Heb. 1:1-2; 2 Tim. 3:16; Eph. 1:17; Acts 26:19; 1 Tim. 1:4; Eph. 1:10; 3:9

OUTLINE

  1. The Triune God:
    1. The source of the divine revelation.
    2. Revealed as the Triune God in the divine revelation throughout the whole Scriptures—Matt. 28:19.
  2. The Triune God’s word:
    1. The speaking God—Heb. 1:1-2.
    2. The Triune God’s speaking became His written word—the holy Scriptures—2 Tim. 3:16.
  3. The Triune God’s revelation:
    1. The Triune God’s word contains and conveys His divine revelation.
    2. Through His Spirit—Eph. 1:17.
  4. The Triune God’s vision:
    1. When the divine light shines over the divine revelation, the divine revelation becomes the divine vision—Acts 26:19.
    2. The Spirit of revelation gives us the sight to see the vision and understand it with His wisdom—Eph. 1:17.
  5. The Triune God’s move:
    1. The Triune God moves in and according to His divine revelation.
    2. For the accomplishment of His eternal economy—1 Tim. 1:4; Eph. 1:10; 3:9.
