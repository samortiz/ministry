CHAPTER THREE

THE LIVING IN THE BODY

Scripture Reading: Col. 1:18; Eph. 4:15-16; Rom. 12:5; Eph. 5:23-24; 1 Tim. 5:17a; 1 Cor. 3:10a; Heb. 13:17; 2 Cor. 3:17; Rom. 8:11, 13; Phil. 1:8; 1 Cor. 12:25b-26

OUTLINE

  1. Living corporately with the members under the Head—Col. 1:18:
    1. Having the Head as the life, the principal object, and the center—Eph. 4:15-16.
    2. Coordinating with the members to live a life which expresses the Head—Rom. 12:5.
  2. Authority in the Body:
    1. The authority of the Head flowing throughout the Body—Eph. 5:23-24.
    2. The deputy authorities carrying out the authority of the Head:
      1. There being elders in the local churches—1 Tim. 5:17a.
      2. There being leading co-workers in the work—1 Cor. 3:10a.
    3. All the members submitting directly to the Head—Eph. 5:24a.
    4. At the same time all the members also submitting to the deputies of the Head—Heb. 13:17.
  3. The authority of the Spirit and the Body:
    1. The Spirit being the embodiment of the Head—2 Cor. 3:17.
    2. The Spirit living and moving in the members—Rom. 8:11, 13.
  4. The members’ feeling for the Body:
    1. Taking the Head’s feeling as their own feeling—Phil. 1:8.
    2. In the principle of caring for the Body—1 Cor. 12:25b-26.
    3. In mind, thoughts, words, and actions, taking the Body as the rule, with no separation or disconnection from the Body.
