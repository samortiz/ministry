Introduction to Nahum:
Author: Nahum (1:1).Time of His Ministry: About 713 B.C., the year in which Sennacherib, the Assyrian king, invaded Judah (2 Kings 18:13-19:37).Place of His Ministry: The southern kingdom of Judah.Object of His Ministry: Nineveh, the capital of Assyria.

Subject of Nahum:
Jehovah's Judgment on Nineveh as the Capital of the Evil Assyria
