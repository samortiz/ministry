1 Your love constrains me,
A tide overflowing
Softens my hard heart,
Erases my care
For You will take me,
Break me, remake me,
An overcomer,
Your kingdom to share.
2 None can compare with You,
Dearest Lord Jesus,
You are the ruler of
The kings of the earth
To You who loved us,
And who has washed us,
From all our sins in
Your own precious blood.
3 Live in life's flowing,
And feed for His growing,
Drop every barrier,
Gaze in His face.
Shout Hallelujah!
Sound out Your victr'y,
You who will live Him
To finish the race.
