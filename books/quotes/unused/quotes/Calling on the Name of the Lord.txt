You will draw water with rejoicing from the springs of salvation, and you will say in that day, Praise Jehovah; call upon His name!
Not only did the Old Testament saints call on the Lord, they even prophesied that others would call on His name
Calling on the name of the Lord was practiced by the New Testament believers beginning on the day of Pentecost
By all of these verses we can see that in the first century the Christians practiced calling on the name of the Lord very much
Throughout the Old Testament as well as in the early days of the Christian age, the saints called on the Lord's name
We believe that today the Lord wants to recover calling on His name and to have us practice it so that we may enjoy the riches of His life
As we have seen earlier, the way to draw water from the springs of salvation is to call upon the name of the Lord
If you have never called, even shouted before the Lord, it is doubtful that you have enjoyed the Lord in a rich way
Another reason for calling on the Lord is to be rescued from distress , from trouble , and from sorrow and pain
Isaiah gives us the way in verse 6 of the same chapter: 'Seek Jehovah while He may be found; call upon Him while He is near'
When we feel that we are down or low, we can lift and stir ourselves up by calling on the name of the Lord Jesus
Timothy 222 says But flee youthful lusts and pursue righteousness faith love peace with those who call on the Lord out of a pure heart
We hope that many more of the Lord's people, especially new believers, will begin the practice of calling on the Lord
