THE TEN GREAT CRITICAL “ONES” FOR THE BUILDING UP OF THE BODY OF CHRIST

MESSAGE FIVE

ONE LIFE-ONE ELEMENT, ONE SALVATION-ONE WAY OF SAVING, AND ONE KIND OF LIVING-ONE MANIFESTATION OF CHRIST

OUTLINE

  1. One life-one element:
    1. The life of the Triune God embodied in Christ-Eph. 4:18.
    2. This life is eternal-John 3:15.
    3. This life is in Christ-John 1:4.
    4. Christ is this life and its Author-John 11:25; 14:6; Acts 3:15.
    5. The Spirit of God is of this life-Rom. 8:2.
    6. This life, which is Christ, is the believers’ life-Col. 3:4.
    7. This life is the divine element of the Triune God and Christ:
      1. God gives His chosen people repentance unto this life and has appointed, ordained, them to receive this life-Acts 11:18; 13:48.
      2. God regenerates the believers with this life-John 1:12; 1 Pet. 1:3.
      3. Christ’s righteous act is unto justification of this life-Rom. 5:18b.
      4. Christ saves His believers subjectively in this life-Rom. 5:10b.
      5. The believers walk in the newness of this life-Rom. 6:4.
      6. The mind of the seeking believers set on the spirit is life and peace-Rom. 8:6b.
      7. Grace reigns through righteousness unto this life-Rom. 5:21.
      8. The overcoming believers reign by the abundant grace and gift of righteousness in this life-Rom. 5:17.
      9. The believers who forsake all for Christ will inherit this life in the kingdom age-Matt. 19:29.
      10. The overcomers will eat of the tree of this life in the kingdom age as a reward-Rev. 2:7.
      11. The tree of this life with the water of this life in the river proceeding from the throne of God will be the unique supply to the entire New Jerusalem and will be partaken of by those who wash their robes-Rev. 22:1-2, 14.
  2. One salvation-one way of saving:
    1. Based upon God’s love (the source of God’s grace) and God’s grace (the expression of God’s love)-Eph. 2:4-5.
    2. Through the death of Christ:
      1. For God’s redemption-Rom. 3:24b.
      2. For God’s forgiveness of sins-Acts 10:43.
      3. For the believers’ reconciliation to God-Rom. 5:10.
      4. For God’s justification-Rom. 3:24a.
    3. Through the life of Christ by the Spirit of life-Rom. 5:10; 8:2:
      1. From regenerating the believers with the divine element of Christ’s life-John 3:5; 1 Pet. 1:3.
      2. Through:
        1. Sanctifying the regenerated believers with the divine element of God’s holy nature-Rom. 6:19, 22.
        2. Renewing the sanctified believers with the newness of life-Titus 3:5; Rom. 6:4.
        3. Transforming the renewed believers with the glorious element of Christ’s life by the Lord Spirit-Rom. 12:2; 2 Cor. 3:18.
        4. Conforming the transformed believers with the divine element of the life of the firstborn Son of God-Rom. 8:29.
      3. Unto the glorification of the conformed believers with the glory of God-Rom. 8:30.
  3. One kind of living-one manifestation of Christ:
    1. By the power of the resurrection of Christ to be conformed to His death (Phil. 3:10), dying to the self, the natural man, the old man, and the flesh (Matt. 16:24; Gal. 2:20; Rom. 6:6; Gal. 5:24) from the power of Satan, the world, and sin (Heb. 2:14; John 12:31; Rom. 6:2).
    2. By the bountiful supply of the Spirit of Jesus Christ to live Christ and magnify Him-Phil. 1:19-21.
    3. By the life of the God-man to live the overcomers’ life-Rev. 2:7, 11, 17, 26; 3:5, 12, 21.
    4. By the growth in the life of Christ unto maturity for the building up of the Body of Christ-Eph. 4:15-16.
