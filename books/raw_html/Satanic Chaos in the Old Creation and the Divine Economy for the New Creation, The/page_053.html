CHAPTER FIVE

THE CHURCH LIFE IN GOD'S ECONOMY

Scripture Reading: Phil. 1:20-21a; John 21:15-17; Rev. 2:4; Phil. 3:12; 1 Cor. 1:9; Col. 1:12, 18b; 1 Tim. 4:6; Eph. 4:3-4, 12, 16; 3:8-10, 2; Acts 8:1; 13:1; Rev. 1:11; John 17:21-23; Rev. 1:12, 20b; 1 Tim. 3:15-16; Rom. 15:16; 1 Pet. 2:5, 9; 1 Cor. 4:15; 1 Thes. 2:7; 1 Pet. 2:2; Heb. 10:24-25; 1 Cor. 14:1-5, 12, 31, 40; Col. 3:10-11

OUTLINE

  1. To live and magnify Christ --Phil. 1:20-21a:
    1. To love and treasure Christ --John 21:15-17; Rev. 2:4.
    2. To pursue and gain Christ --Phil. 3:12.
    3. To enjoy and partake of Christ --1 Cor. 1:9; Col. 1:12.
    4. To exalt and minister Christ --Col. 1:18b; 1 Tim. 4:6.
  2. To build up the Body of Christ --Eph. 4:12:
    1. To keep the universal oneness of the Body --Eph. 4:3-4.
    2. By all the members of the Body, perfected in the local churches --Eph. 4:12:
      1. Either as the joints of the rich supply of Christ.
      2. Or as parts of the Body operating in their measure --Eph. 4:16.
    3. With the unsearchable riches of Christ --Eph. 3:8-10.
    4. Through the divine dispensing --Eph. 3:2.
  3. To meet as a proper local church --Acts 8:1; 13:1; Rev. 1:11:
    1. On the genuine ground of the unique oneness of the Body --Eph. 4:3-4; John 17:21-23.
    2. As a local expression of the universal Body of Christ.
    3. As a local golden lampstand --Rev. 1:12, 20b.
    4. As the pillar and base of the truth for the manifestation of God in the flesh --the great mystery of godliness --1 Tim. 3:15-16.
    5. Meeting and serving in mutuality according to the God-ordained way:
      1. Fulfilling the New Testament gospel priesthood for begetting --Rom. 15:16; 1 Pet. 2:5, 9; 1 Cor. 4:15.
      2. Nourishing and cherishing the new believers in home meetings --John 21:15; 1 Thes. 2:7; 1 Pet. 2:2.
      3. Perfecting the saints in group meetings that they may be able to do the work of the ministry, that is, to build up the Body of Christ --Eph. 4:12; Heb. 10:24-25.
      4. Prophesying in the church meetings for the building up of the Body of Christ through the local churches --1 Cor. 14:1-5, 12, 31.
    6. Having the adequate and proper leadership for the keeping of a good order (1 Cor. 14:40) with the all-inclusive Christ as the center and the processed Triune God as the manifestation --Col. 3:10-11; 1 Tim. 3:15-16.
