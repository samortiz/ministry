PREFACE

This book is composed of messages given by Brother Witness Lee in the Philippines. Chapters 1, 2, 4, 5, and 6 were given in Manila and were published in Chinese in The Ministry of the Word in 1959 and 1960. Chapter 3 has been previously published in English as the booklet The God of Resurrection.
