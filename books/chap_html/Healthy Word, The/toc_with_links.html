PREFACE

This book is composed of messages shared by Brother Witness Lee in Atlanta, Georgia, in Miami, Florida, and in Toronto, Canada, during September of 1978. Chapters 1 through 3 were shared in Atlanta on September 5 through 7. Chapters 4 through 6 were shared in Miami on September 8 through 10. Chapters 7 through 9 were shared in Toronto on September 12 through 14.
