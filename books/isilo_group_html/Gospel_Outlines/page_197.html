Subject One Hundred Ninety-six

THE LORD’S BLOOD CLEANSES US FROM SIN

Scripture: 1 John 1:7

  1. The Lord Jesus bore our sins on the cross and shed His blood for us
  2. The function of the Lord’s blood
    1. Because Jesus bore our sins and shed His blood, His blood can cleanse us from our sins.
    2. The cleansing of the Lord’s blood is always effective.
  3. The meaning of being cleansed from sins
    1. The Lord’s blood cleanses us from our sins; that is, it erases our record of sins before God.
    2. When we are cleansed by the Lord’s blood, all record of our sins is erased before God.
  4. How to obtain the cleansing of the Lord’s blood
    Develop this point.
