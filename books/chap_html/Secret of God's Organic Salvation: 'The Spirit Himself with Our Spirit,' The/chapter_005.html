CHAPTER FIVE

THE SECRET OF CONFORMATION
AND GLORIFICATION

OUTLINE

  1. The secret of conformation—the seventh section of God’s organic salvation:
    1. God’s intention is to make man like Him in His divine life, nature, and image as His expression, but not in His Godhead.
    2. God regenerates the believers in Christ with His divine life that they may begin to participate in His divinity.
    3. After being regenerated, the regenerated believers begin to grow through:
      1. The divine feeding,
      2. The divine sanctification,
      3. The divine renewing,
      4. The divine transformation in the divine life,
    4. Until they mature in the divine life by the maturing Spirit in the believers’ spirit enriched with Christ to be a full-grown man, at the measure of the stature of the fullness of Christ—Col. 1:28; Eph. 4:13,
    5. Conformed to the image of Christ the firstborn Son of God—Rom. 8:29,
    6. Who was begotten by God in His resurrection with both His divinity and His enlivened and uplifted humanity, possessing two natures, both divine and human—Acts 13:33; Rom. 1:4; 1 Pet. 3:18.
    7. Thus, the conformation of the believers is the consummation of their transformation in the divine life through which they participate in God’s divinity in full.
  2. The secret of glorification—the eighth section of God’s organic salvation:
    1. In regeneration God seals the regenerated believers with His Spirit—Eph. 1:13.
    2. This sealing of the sealing Spirit is like inking, saturating the sealed believers from within with the glorious life element of God throughout their life, resulting in the redemption of their body— Eph. 4:30; Rom. 8:23.
    3. At the time of the rapture of the believers who are matured in the divine life, they will be brought in their spirit exulting with Christ from without into the glory of God for their glorification—Rom. 8:30; Heb. 2:10.
    4. Thus, the matured believers will be glorified from within through the lifelong saturation with the glory of God and from without through their being brought into God’s glory.
    5. The glorification of the matured believers is their top portion of their divine sonship in God’s organic salvation—which they received at the time of their regeneration—Gal. 4:5; Rom. 8:23.
    6. The redemption of the believers’ body is the transfiguration of their body at the Lord’s coming back—Phil. 3:20-21.
    7. The word redemption indicates it is judicial because the believers’ redeemed body is of the fallen old creation—this redemption of the believers’ body is the finalization of God’s judicial redemption; the word transfiguration indicates it is organic because the transfiguration of the believers’ body is by the divine life within them.
    8. The divine glorification of the glorified believers makes the consummated believers participate in God’s divinity to the uttermost.
