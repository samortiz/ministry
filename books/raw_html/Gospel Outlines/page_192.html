Subject One Hundred Ninety-one

JESUS IS UNCHANGING

Scripture: Heb. 13:8

  1. Jesus is God
    1. Prove that Jesus is God.
    2. As God is eternal and unchanging, so Jesus is unchanging.
  2. Jesus is eternal
    1. Jesus died and rose again.
    2. Jesus lives today and will be living forever.
    3. What Jesus did in the past He is doing today, and in the future He will still be doing the same.
    4. Likewise, what Jesus was able to do in the past He is able to do now, and will still be able to do in eternity.
  3. Jesus is real
    1. As Jesus was so real to people when He was on the earth, so He is also real to men today.
    2. Jesus is real because He is living. Because Jesus is living forever, He will be real forever.
    3. Since Jesus is real, the deliverance and healing He gives to us is real.
  4. How to partake of this unchanging Jesus
    Develop this point.
