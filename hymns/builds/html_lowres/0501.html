Col 1:15
1 O glorious Christ, Savior mine, 2 Tim 1:10
Thou art truly radiance divine;
God infinite, in eternity,
Yet man in time, finite to be. John 7:6
Oh! Christ, expression of God, the Great, Col 1:15
Inexhaustible, rich, and sweet!
God mingled with humanity John 10:30
Lives in me my all to be. Gal 2:20 Col 3:11
2 The fulness of God dwells in Thee; Col 2:9
Thou dost manifest God's glory; John 17:4
In flesh Thou hast redemption wrought; Rom 3:24
As Spirit, oneness with me sought.
3 All things of the Father are Thine; John 16:15
All Thou art in Spirit is mine;
The Spirit makes Thee real to me, John 16:13
That Thou experienced might be. John 15:26 John 14:17
4 The Spirit of life causes Thee Rom 8:29
By Thy Word to transfer to me.
Thy Spirit touched, Thy Word received, John 1:12
Thy life in me is thus conceived.
5 In spirit while gazing on Thee, 2 Cor 3:18
As a glass reflecting Thy glory,
Like to Thyself transformed I'll be,
That Thou might be expressed thru me.
6 In no other way could we be
Sanctified and share Thy vict'ry; John 17:19
Thus only spiritual we'll be,
And touch the life of glory.
7 Thy Spirit will me saturate,
Every part will God permeate, Eph 3:17
Deliv'ring me from the old man, Rom 6:6
With all saints building for His plan. Eph 4:12-13
