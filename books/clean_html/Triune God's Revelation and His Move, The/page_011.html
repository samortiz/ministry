THE TRIUNE GOD’S REVELATION AND HIS MOVE

MESSAGE FOUR

THE WAY TO RECEIVE REVELATION FROM THE HOLY WORD AND SEE VISION THROUGH REVELATION

Scripture Reading: Eph. 6:17-18; 1:17-18; Acts 26:16-19; 16:9-10; 1:8; 10:17-20

OUTLINE

  1. The way to receive revelation from the holy Word:
    1. To read the holy Word and apprehend its contents in facts:
      1. To be familiar with the text.
      2. By the help of the original text—Hebrew and Greek.
      3. By learning to know the types—figures and shadows.
    2. To receive the divine revelation by exercising our spirit to contact the Lord through pray-reading—Eph. 6:17-18:
      1. Not by our natural understanding ability.
      2. Not by our fanciful imaginations.
      3. But by the spirit of wisdom and revelation—Eph. 1:17.
      4. Through much prayer in contacting the Lord.
  2. The way to see vision through the divine revelation:
    1. Through the enlightenment of the divine light—Eph. 1:18b.
    2. By contacting the Lord through the reviewing of the divine revelation in our spirit.
    3. By having the spiritual sight—Eph. 1:18a.
    4. The divine vision is mostly concerning God’s move—Acts 26:16-19; 16:9-10; 1:8; 10:17-20.
