Lesson Fifteen

THE BUILDING UP OF THE BODY OF CHRIST IN ONENESS

Scripture Reading

1 Cor. 8:4; Isa. 45:5; John 17:20-23; Eph. 2:15; Rom. 15:7; Phil. 2:2; 1 Cor. 12:25; Psa. 133:1-3

Outline

  1. God being one
    1. In His essence
    2. In His purpose
    3. In His economy
    4. In His acts
  2. The unique God having a unique expression
  3. The Lord’s aspiration and prayer concerning the oneness of the Body
  4. The Lord’s accomplishment of the oneness of the Body
  5. The fulfillment of the oneness of the Body
  6. The practice of the oneness of the Body universally
    1. Receiving the teaching and fellowship of the apostles
    2. Staying in fellowship with all the churches
  7. The practice of the oneness of the Body locally
    1. On the ground of oneness
    2. Being one with all the saints
    3. Submitting to the authority of the elders
