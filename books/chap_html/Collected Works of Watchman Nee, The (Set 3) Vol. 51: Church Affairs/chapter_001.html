PREFACE

Volume 51 of The Collected Works contains messages given by Watchman Nee in a training for co-workers and elders at Kuling Mountain in China in 1948. These messages were originally published in Chinese. The English edition is based on the fourth edition of the book which was printed in December 1982 by the Juridical Party of Taiwan Gospel Book Room. The contents of Volume 51 have been published previously by Living Stream Ministry under the title Church Affairs.
