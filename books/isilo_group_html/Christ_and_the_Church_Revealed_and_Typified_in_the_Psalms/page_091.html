CONCERNING THE EARTH

Now let us consider briefly the aspects of the earth as seen in the Psalms. The word earth appears about 190 times altogether in the Psalms.

  1. The earth has been given to Christ as an inheritance-Psalm 2:8
  2. His name will be excellent in all the earth-Psalm 8:1
  3. The earth is the Lord’s and the fulness thereof-Psalm 24:1
  4. The earth was created by the Lord-Psalm 24:2
  5. Christ will come back to take the earth-Psalm 96:13
  6. Christ will reign over the earth-Psalm 72:8
  7. Christ will make His saints princes in all the earth-Psalm 45:16
  8. The earth will turn to Christ-Psalm 22:27
  9. The earth will remember Christ-Psalm 22:27
  10. The earth will worship Christ-Psalm 22:27, 29
  11. The earth will praise Christ-Psalm 98:4
  12. The whole earth will be filled with His glory-Psalm 72:19

The main Psalms concerning the earth: Psalms 47, 68, 72, 89, 145, 146, 148, 149
