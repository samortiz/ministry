HYMN NO. 83

8.6.8.6.6.6.7.5. with chorus.

As I am fighting for my Lord,
    The devil won't give in;
Satan with dark authority
    Resists when I begin.
    But as I pray with might
    That evil would take flight,
Calling, talking with my Lord,
    All things will be right.

(Chorus)
    To call on Him and talk to Him
        Makes all things right;
    To call on Him and talk to Him
        Makes all things right.
    Praise to my Lord, whene'er
    I would the least despair—
    To call on Him and talk to Him
        Makes all things right.

Passing through dark, uncertain ways,
    Facing torrent and wind,
Though bitter hardship be my lot,
    Met with apparent end,
    There's victory in sight,
    An Overcomer's might;
He's the One I call upon,
    All things will be right.

When homefolks are accusing me,
    Loved ones' contact lost,
When I am lonely, poor, and weak
    And by rejection tossed,
    To Him I bring my need,
    I'm comforted indeed.
Truly when I come to Him,
    All things will be right.

Since often I converse with Him,
    No anxious cares molest;
Daily I can rejoice and sing,
    Lean on His loving breast.
    I'll rest upon His might
    Until the end's in sight;
Just to call and talk to Him,
    All things will be right.
