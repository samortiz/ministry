HYMN NO. 179

9.9.6.6.6.4

"Almost persuaded," now to believe;
"Almost persuaded," Christ to receive;
    Seems now some soul to say,
    "Go, Spirit, go Thy way,
    Some more convenient day
    On Thee I'll call."

"Almost persuaded," come, come today;
"Almost persuaded," turn not away;
    Jesus invites you here,
    Angels are ling'ring near,
    Prayers rise from hearts so dear,
    O wand'rer, come.

"Almost persuaded," harvest is past!
"Almost persuaded," doom comes at last!
    "Almost" cannot avail;
    "Almost" is but to fail!
    Sad, sad, that bitter wail,
    "Almost," but lost!

Please be persuaded, He is so good;
Please be persuaded, trust in His blood;
    Jesus forgives your sins,
    All vileness He can cleanse;
    His death your only means,
    Open your heart.

Now be persuaded; please, sinner, come.
Now be persuaded, make Christ your home.
    Jesus is calling you,
    Yield, all you need to do;
    Blessing will come anew,
    Believe Him now.

Stanzas 1 through 3 written by P. P. Bliss.
1951 Inspiring Hymns, Alfred B. Smith, Singspiration, Inc.
Zondervan Publishing House, Grand Rapids, Michigan.
