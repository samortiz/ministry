CRYSTALLIZATION-STUDY
OF THE GOSPEL OF JOHN

MESSAGE NINE

THE CONSUMMATION
OF THE COMPLETE SALVATION OF CHRIST

OUTLINE

  1. The church, the Body of Christ, as the bride, the wife, of Christ is the consummation of His complete salvation by His ministry in three stages with the twenty all-inclusive items of what He is, from God to the life-giving Spirit:
    1. By the judicial redemption accomplished by Christ as the flesh in His incarnation.
    2. By His organic salvation carried out by Him as the life-giving Spirit in His inclusion.
    3. By the intensified work of His organic salvation by Him as the sevenfold intensified Spirit in His intensification.
  2. The bride, the wife, of Christ will be prepared in full by Him for His wedding as His pleasure and satisfaction at the opening of the kingdom age:
    1. The kingdom age will be one thousand years as one wedding day to celebrate the exultant wedding of Christ.
    2. This excellent consummation, the bride, the wife, of Christ, of His complete salvation will be the New Jerusalem in its initial consummation for the celebration of Christ's wedding for one thousand years.
    3. It will be for Christ's eternal enjoyment in its ultimate consummation in the new heaven and new earth for eternity.
