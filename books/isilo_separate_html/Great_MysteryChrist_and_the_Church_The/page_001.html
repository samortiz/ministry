PREFACE

This book is composed of messages given by Brother Witness Lee in Hong Kong, Taipei, and Tokyo from October 30 to December 1, 1981. The messages in this book are compiled into four sections. Chapters 1 through 4 form Section 1 and were given in a conference in Hong Kong from October 30 through November 8. Chapters 5 through 8 form Section 2 and were given in a conference in Taipei from November 15 through 22. Chapters 9 through 12 form Section 3 and were given in a conference in Tokyo from November 27 through 30. Chapters 13 through 16 form Section 4. This section is composed of messages and fellowship given in Taipei and Tokyo on November 25 and 26 and on December 1. Chapters 1 through 13 were published in Chinese in The Ministry of the Word in 1982 through 1984.
