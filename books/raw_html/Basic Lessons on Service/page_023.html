LESSON FIVE

THE PRACTICE OF
THE LORD'S TABLE MEETING

(2)

Scripture Reading: Matt. 26:30; Heb. 2:12; Eph. 2:18; John 4:23-24

OUTLINE

  1. Worshipping the Father:
    1. After eating the bread and drinking the cup—Matt. 26:26-30.
    2. By praising—Matt. 26:30:
      1. Christ, the Firstborn of God, taking the lead among His brothers—Heb. 2:12.
      2. We, the many sons of God, following Christ in praising the Father.
    3. Addressing praise to the Father:
      1. Through the Son.
      2. In the Spirit—Eph. 2:18.
    4. Calling hymns:
      1. According to the Father's being.
      2. According to the Father's attributes.
    5. By presenting Christ to God the Father:
      1. As the peace offering—Lev. 3.
      2. Enjoying Him before God the Father—Lev. 7:14-21, 28-34:
        1. As life supply—vv. 14-16.
        2. As love in resurrection—vv. 29-31.
        3. As power in ascension—vv. 32-34.
        4. In cleanness—vv. 17-21.

Focus: The worshipping of the Father should be with the Son and in the Spirit (John 4:23-24).
