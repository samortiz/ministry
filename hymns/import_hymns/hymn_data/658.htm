Hymns, #658

Encouragement - For Walking in the Light

E658 C- K- S- T-

Lyrics:Barton
Music:Handley C. G. Moule
Meter:8.6.8.6

Hymns using same tune:

#414, #424 (Tune 2)

Verses:  1   2   3   4   5   6 

 
 1 

Walk in the light, and thou shalt know

That fellowship of love

His Spirit only can bestow,

Who reigns in light above.
 
 2 

Walk in the light, and thou shalt find

Thy heart made truly His;

Who dwells in cloudless light enshrined,

In whom no darkness is.
 
 3 

Walk in the light, and sin abhorred

Shall ne'er defile again;

The blood of Jesus Christ the Lord

Shall cleanse from every stain.
 
 4 

Walk in the light, and e'en the tomb

No fearful shade shall wear;

The glory shall dispel the gloom,

For Christ hath conquered there.
 
 5 

Walk in the light, and thou shalt own

Thy darkness passed away,

Because that light hath on thee shone

In which is perfect day.
 
 6 

Walk in the light, thy path shall be

Serene and clear and bright;

For God, by grace, shall dwell in thee,

And God Himself is Light.
