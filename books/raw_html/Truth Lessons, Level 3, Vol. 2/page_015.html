LESSON TWENTY-FIVE

HUMAN UNCLEANNESS—
HUMAN BIRTH, LEPROSY, AND DISCHARGES

OUTLINE

  1. Human birth:
    1. Woman’s relationship to human birth:
      1. Woman representing all mankind.
      2. The human birth coming out of the woman being unclean.
    2. The uncleanness of the male’s birth.
    3. The uncleanness of the female’s birth.
    4. The way for man to be cleansed from human uncleanness:
      1. Being circumcised:
        1. The cutting off of man’s natural being.
        2. Carried out on the eighth day.
      2. Offering Christ as the burnt offering and the sin offering:
        1. The burnt offering.
        2. The sin offering.
  2. Leprosy:
    1. The origin of leprosy.
    2. The nature of leprosy.
    3. The result of leprosy.
    4. Leprosy signifying the sin that dwells in man.
    5. The leper:
      1. Tearing his clothes, having his hair let loose, and covering his upper lip and crying “Unclean, unclean!”
      2. Dwelling alone outside the camp.
  3. Discharges:
    1. All discharges from the human body being unclean.
    2. The way for cleansing a person who has a discharge:
      1. Washing the clothes and bathing the body in running water.
      2. Offering a sin offering and a burnt offering on the eighth day.
