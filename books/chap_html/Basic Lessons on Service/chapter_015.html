LESSON FIFTEEN

TAKING HEED HOW TO BUILD

Scripture Reading: 1 Cor. 3:10-15

OUTLINE

  1. The unique foundation having been laid—vv. 10a, 11.
  2. Building on the unique foundation—v. 10m.
  3. Building with gold, silver, and precious stones—v. 12a:
    1. Gold symbolizing God's nature.
    2. Silver symbolizing Christ's redemptive work.
    3. Precious stones symbolizing the Spirit's transforming work.
  4. Building with wood, grass, and stubble—v. 12b:
    1. Wood signifying human nature.
    2. Grass signifying man in the flesh—Isa. 40:6-7.
    3. Stubble signifying lifelessness.
  5. Every man's work will be tested by fire—vv. 13-15:
    1. If any man's work stands the fire, he will receive a reward.
    2. If any man's work is consumed, he will suffer loss:
      1. He himself will be saved.
      2. Yet so as through fire.
