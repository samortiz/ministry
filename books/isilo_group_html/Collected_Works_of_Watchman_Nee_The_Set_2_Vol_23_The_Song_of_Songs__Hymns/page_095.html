HYMN NO. 79

8.4.8.8.4. with chorus.

Crucified was my Lord Jesus,
    I too was there.
He descended far and reached me
That His member I could now be,
    His life to share.

(Chorus)
    Crucified with Christ my Savior;
        'Tis His living, 'tis no longer I.
    Though I now live in my body,
    Christ Himself is living in me.
        He so loved me, He for me has died;
    We're joined as one, my God and I.

When my Lord was resurrected,
    I too was there.
As a branch with tree is living,
Resurrection I am sharing,
    His image bear.

When He comes to claim His chosen,
    Through rapture fine,
I will match Him, I'll be with Him,
Know His blessings, ever taste them,
    His honor mine.

In His life I am transcendent,
    Oh, keep me, Lord.
In His keeping I am trusting
Daily whether living, dying,
    His worth accord.

 

HYMN NO. 80

8.6.8.6.

The battle is not ours to fight,
    The struggle we don't need.
True faith is always at full rest,
    From woes and struggles freed.

Our God will fight the enemy,
    He truly cares for us,
And through His power firmly stand
    No fear need be in us.

The battles we encounter here
    On Calvary were won;
Hold fast the ground, His pow'r apply,
    All work has now been done.
