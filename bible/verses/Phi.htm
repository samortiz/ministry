Introduction to Philippians:
Author: Paul with Timothy, slaves of Christ Jesus (1:1).Time of Writing: Approximately A.D. 64, perhaps after Ephesians was written (cf. 1:25; 2:24).Place of Writing: Rome, where Paul was imprisoned (1:13; Acts 28:30).Recipients: The saints in Philippi, with the overseers and deacons (1:1).

Subject of Philippians:
Experiencing Christ—
Taking Christ as Our Living, Pattern, Goal, Power, and Secret
