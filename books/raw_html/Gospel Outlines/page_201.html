Subject Two Hundred

HE THAT HAS THE SON HAS LIFE

Scripture: 1 John 5:11-13

  1. The Son of God is God in another form
    1. The Son of God is Jesus Christ. Jesus Christ is God come as a man in order to save man.
    2. The Son of God is God made manifest to man.
  2. God’s life is in the Son of God
    1. God is in His Son, so God’s life is also in the Son.
    2. The life that is in the Son of God is the eternal life.
  3. He that has the Son of God has life
    1. Life is within the Son of God. If man wants to obtain life, he must receive the Son of God.
    2. Once man receives the Son of God, he has God and also the life of God.
  4. The proof of having life
    1. 1 John 5:12 says, “He that hath the Son hath the life.”
    2. The proof that someone has obtained life is that his living has changed. Describe how once a man receives the Lord, his life is changed.
  5. How to obtain the Son of God
    1. Confess that you do not have God’s life.
    2. Believe in the Son of God.
    3. To believe means to receive the Son of God as Savior with our heart and our spirit.
