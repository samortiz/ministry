1 Oh tell me, what's the key
To build the Body?
How to be deified,
The Body realize?
Eat the hidden manna-
Get God into you,
'Cause when God gets into you,
You get into God!
2 Our God is ent'rable,
And He's enjoyable,
Our Christ-He is the way,
We eat Him ev'ry day.
3 Center of centers 'tis,
The hidden manna is
Our deepest life supply;
How He does satisfy!
4 Don't trust the circumstance,
For it is just the chance.
To get more God in you,
Become Him through and through!
5 Spend time alone with Him,
Just love Him, talk to Him;
By this we're deified
And we become His Bride!
