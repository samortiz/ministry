Introduction to Jeremiah:
Author: Jeremiah (1:1).Time of Writing: The late seventh and early sixth centuries B.C.Place of Writing: Jerusalem and Egypt.Time Period Covered: This book covers a period of at least forty-two years, from 628 B.C., the thirteenth year of Josiah's reign (1:2), until some time after 586 B.C., after Gedaliah the governor of Judah was murdered (41:2) and Jeremiah was carried away to Egypt (43:6-8).

Subject of Jeremiah:
Christ Being Made the Righteousness of Jehovah
to God's Elect as Their Center and Circumference,
in God's Dealings with Israel and the Nations
