Introduction to Hosea:
Author: Hosea (1:1).Time of His Ministry: A period of about sixty years, from approximately 785 B.C., during the reign of Uzziah (Azariah), king of Judah, to approximately 725 B.C., during the reign of Hezekiah, king of Judah (1:1).Place of His Ministry: The northern kingdom of Israel.Object of His Ministry: The northern kingdom of Israel.

Subject of Hosea:
Jehovah as Salvation to the Adulterous and Apostate Israel
in Receiving Her Back and Restoring Her
