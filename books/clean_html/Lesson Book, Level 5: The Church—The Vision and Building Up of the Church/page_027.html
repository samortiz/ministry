Lesson Six

THE STATUS OF THE CHURCH (4)

THE GOLDEN LAMPSTANDS

Scripture Reading

Exo. 25:31-40; Zech. 3:9; 4:2-10; Rev. 1:11-12, 20b; 4:5; 5:6; Col. 2:9; 1:15; Gen. 1:26; 1 John 5:11-12; Rev. 21:18b, 23; 22:1, 5

Outline

  1. The golden lampstands typifying Christ as the embodiment of the Triune God
    1. Gold signifying the Father’s divine nature, the divine substance, essence
    2. The form signifying the Son as the embodiment of the Godhead in His humanity
    3. The seven lamps signifying the Spirit as the expression
  2. The golden lampstands signifying Israel as God’s testimony in the Old Testament and in the millennium
    1. Shining through the Seven Eyes (Spirits) of Jehovah
    2. As the Seven Eyes of Christ (the Stone-Savior for God’s Building)
  3. The golden lampstands signifying the seven local churches as God’s testimony in the New Testament
    1. Shining in the dark age as in the night, bearing the testimony of Jesus
      1. With the Father’s divine essence
      2. In the Son’s human form
      3. Through the Spirit’s expression
    2. To be consummated in the New Jerusalem
    3. Expressing the processed Triune God in eternity
