What a wonderful change in my life has been wrought

Since Jesus came into my heart!

I have light in my soul for which long I had sought,

Since Jesus came into my heart!
 

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Since Jesus came into my heart!
 

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Floods of joy o'er my soul like the sea billows roll,

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Floods of joy o'er my soul like the sea billows roll,

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Since Jesus came into my heart!

Floods of joy o'er my soul like the sea billows roll,

Since Jesus came into my heart!
