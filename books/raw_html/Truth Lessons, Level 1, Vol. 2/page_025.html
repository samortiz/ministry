LESSON SEVENTEEN

A KEY VIEW OF THE NEW TESTAMENT

(1)

JOHN THE BAPTIST

OUTLINE

  1. The background.
  2. His status.
  3. His birth:
    1. Conceived.
    2. Born.
    3. Circumcised and named.
  4. His life:
    1. Living in the wilderness.
    2. His way of life.
  5. His teaching:
    1. To repent and produce fruits.
    2. To live a superior life.
  6. His ministry:
    1. Preaching the baptism of repentance.
    2. Bringing men to receive Christ:
      1. Preparing men’s hearts to receive Christ.
      2. Ushering men to Christ.
  7. His type.
  8. His disciples:
    1. Making his teaching into a religion.
    2. Causing his work to be in rivalry with Christ.
  9. His ending.
