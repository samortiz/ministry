Subject Two Hundred Fifty-six

THE SATISFACTION OF MAN

Scripture: John 4:14; 6:35; 7:38

  1. Man’s satisfaction is not found in material things
    Even after a man has accumulated great wealth, he is not satisfied.
  2. Man’s satisfaction is not found in psychological things
    Even after man has received honor and fame, he is still not satisfied.
  3. Man’s real satisfaction is found in his spirit
    This satisfaction comes when God enters into man’s spirit. Only God can satisfy the human spirit.

Subject Two Hundred Fifty-seven

THE REAL LIFE

Scripture: Eph. 3:19

  1. God is man’s true life
    Since God is the meaning of life and the satisfaction of man, He is the true life of a man. If a man is without God, not only is his an empty life, but also a false life.
  2. The real life is one that is filled with God inwardly
  3. The real life is one that is absolutely living for God
  4. The real life is also the living out of God Himself

Subject Two Hundred Fifty-eight

HOW TO RECEIVE GOD
AND MANIFEST HIS LIFE

Scripture: Acts 26:20; John 1:12-13; Rom. 12:1-2

  1. Confess that you need God
  2. Repent and turn to God
  3. Receive the Son of God as your Savior
  4. Consecrate yourself to God
  5. Practice being one spirit with the Lord
