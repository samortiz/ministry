THE TEN GREAT CRITICAL “ONES” FOR THE BUILDING UP OF THE BODY OF CHRIST

MESSAGE ONE

ONE BIBLE-ONE REVELATION AND ONE GOD-ONE ECONOMY

Scripture Reading: 2 Pet. 1:20-21

OUTLINE

  1. One Bible-one revelation:
    1. The unique Bible is consistent in its unique revelation:
      1. It should not be interpreted according to man’s own concepts-understandings, logic, philosophies, views, and fanciful imaginations-2 Pet. 1:20-21.
      2. Any interpretation of the unique Bible should be governed by the principles of its interpretation:
        1. The unique Bible should be interpreted only by and according to itself.
        2. It should be interpreted by discerning the differences in the dispensational progress of the divine revelation.
        3. Any interpretation of the unique Bible should take care of its minor revelations, but should not have any minor revelations interpreted against, not in line or in harmony with, the major revelation of this unique and consistent Bible.
    2. The unique, major revelation of the unique Bible is the unique contents of God’s economy (see point II. D.).
  2. One God-one economy:
    1. God is self-existing and ever-existing-Exo. 3:14.
    2. God is triune-the Father, the Son, and the Spirit-Matt. 28:19; 2 Cor. 13:14.
    3. The Triune God has one heart’s desire-Eph. 1:5, 9.
    4. According to His heart’s desire, God made His eternal economy-1 Tim. 1:4b; Eph. 1:10; 3:9:
      1. To make man the same as He is in life and nature but not in His Godhead.
      2. To make Himself one with man and man one with Him, thus to be enlarged and expanded in His expression, that all His divine attributes may be expressed in human virtues.
      3. Through:
        1. Creating man in His image and after His likeness-Gen. 1:26-27.
        2. Becoming man in His incarnation to partake of the human nature-Heb. 2:14a.
        3. Living a human life to express His attributes through man’s virtues.
        4. Dying an all-inclusive death.
        5. Resurrecting to produce the firstborn Son of God and become the life-giving Spirit-Rom. 8:29; Acts 13:33; 1 Cor. 15:45.
        6. Dispensing Himself into His chosen people to regenerate them with Himself as their life for producing many sons-many God-men-1 Pet. 1:3.
        7. Forming the churches with His many sons.
        8. Building up the Body of Christ with His brothers as the members to be the organism of the processed and consummated Triune God.
        9. Consummating in the New Jerusalem as His eternal enlargement and expression.
