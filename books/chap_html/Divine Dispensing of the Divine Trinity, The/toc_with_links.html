PREFACE

This book is composed of messages given by Brother Witness Lee in 1983. Chapters one through ten, covering the central view of the divine dispensation, were given in Stuttgart, Germany in May. Chapters eleven through fourteen, covering the divine dispensing of the Divine Trinity, were given in New York City in June. Chapters fifteen through twenty are concerning the divine dispensing of the Divine Trinity in Ephesians and were given in Irving, Texas in June. Chapters twenty-one through twenty-four cover the divine dispensing of the Divine Trinity in Romans and were given in Berkeley, California in July. Finally, chapters twenty-five through forty-three, covering the divine dispensing of the Divine Trinity in 1 and 2 Corinthians, were given in Irving, Texas in December.
