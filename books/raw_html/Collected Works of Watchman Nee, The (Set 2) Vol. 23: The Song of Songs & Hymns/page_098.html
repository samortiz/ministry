HYMN NO. 84

8.7.8.7.

Jesus, Lord, Thou art my shepherd;
    Thee I thank and praise indeed!
By such grace that Thou hast given,
    I have neither want nor need.

Now I lie in Thy green pastures,
    Finding rest and peace secure;
Satisfied upon Thy bosom,
    I will hunger nevermore.

Thou to restful waters leadest,
    Life's pure waters flowing free;
Of its sweetness I am tasting,
    Quenched my thirst effectively.

Full of joy, I live before Thee
    Since Thou hast restored my soul;
In sure righteous paths Thou leadest
    For Thy name's sake, for Thy goal.

Through the vale of death's dark shadow
    From all fear I know I'm free;
Rod and staff of Thine both comfort,
    For I know Thou art with me.

Lo, a table spread before me
    In the presence of my foes;
Thou my head with oil anointest,
    And my cup with joy o'erflows.

All my life's days, goodness, mercy
    By design shall follow me;
In Jehovah's habitation
    I will dwell eternally.

 

HYMN NO. 85

8.7.8.7. (Hymns, #127)

Hark! ten thousand voices crying,
    "Lamb of God!" with one accord;
Thousand thousand saints replying,
    Wake at once the echo'ng chord.

"Praise the Lamb!" the chorus waking,
    All in heav'n together throng;
Loud and far each tongue partaking
    Rolls around the endless song.

Grateful incense this, ascending
    Ever to the Father's throne;
Every knee to Jesus bending,
    All the mind in heav'n is one.

All the Father's counsels claiming
    Equal honors to the Son,
All the Son's effulgence beaming,
    Makes the Father's glory known.

By the Spirit all pervading,
    Hosts unnumbered round the Lamb,
Crowned with light and joy unfading,
    Hail Him as the great "I AM."

Joyful now the new creation
    Rests in undisturbed repose,
Blest in Jesus' full salvation,
    Sorrow now nor thraldom knows.

Hark! the heavenly notes again!
    Loudly swells the song of praise;
Through creation's vault, Amen!
    Amen! responsive joy doth raise.
