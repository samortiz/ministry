Introduction to Zephaniah:
Author: Zephaniah (1:1).Time of His Ministry: About 630 B.C., at the time of Habakkuk and Jeremiah, not long before the beginning of the Babylonian captivity (605 B.C.).Place of His Ministry: Judah.Object of His Ministry: The territory of Judah and Jerusalem, the capital of Judah.

Subject of Zephaniah:
Jehovah's Judgment on Israel and on the Nations
and His Salvation to the Gentiles and to Israel
