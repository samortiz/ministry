HYMN NO. 57

7.6.7.6.

Satan's assaults are conquered,
    The Lamb's dear blood our shield;
These words of testimony
    The fearful sword we wield.

The devil we are shaming
    By hiding in the cross,
This proves that God has judged him,
    And bound his work as dross.

The enemy of God, he's
    Our enemy as well.
The curse of God has judged him;
    It cannot be dispelled.

You warriors of darkness,
    So crafty, hideous,
We now expose your nature;
    You have no room in us.

You are the filth of Hades,
    Whatever name you own;
Our spirit, soul, and body
    Will not spare you a throne.

Your words and accusation
    We do not want the least;
We choose to be perfected
    By God who is our feast.

Ruler of darkness, notice
    By pow'r of Calvary
Your self-made plan is fallen,
    And you are forced to flee.

Satan, we hate you ever,
    We fight you unto death;
Christ's blood announced the judgment
    We execute by faith.
