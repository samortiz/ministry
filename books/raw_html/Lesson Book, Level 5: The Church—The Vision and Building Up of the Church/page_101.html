Lesson Seventeen

THE MEETINGS OF THE CHURCH FOR THE BUILDING UP OF THE CHURCH

Scripture Reading

Heb. 10:25; Col. 1:2; Acts 20:7; 1 Cor. 11:20, 23-25; 14:23, 26; Matt. 18:19-20; Acts 2:46; Col. 3:16; 1 Cor. 6:17; 2 Cor. 3:17; Eph. 5:18-19

Outline

  1. The church meetings are ordained by God for the believers
  2. The believers are a meeting people
  3. The purpose of the church meetings—to exhibit Christ in all the saints
  4. The goal of the church meetings
  5. The types of church meetings
    1. Meeting to break bread
    2. Meeting to pray
    3. Meeting to exercise the spiritual gifts
    4. Meeting to read the Word
    5. Meeting to listen to messages
  6. The size of the church meetings
    1. In the believers’ homes
    2. In a larger meeting place
  7. Examples of the meetings in the New Testament
    1. The first meeting of the church before Pentecost
    2. The first meetings of the church after Pentecost
  8. How to meet
    1. Being gathered into the Lord’s name
    2. Meeting with the basic factors and elements—the Word, the Spirit, praying, and singing
