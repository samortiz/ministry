HYMN NO. 124

8.8.8.8.

Now we are resting in the Lamb—
    Who can enough His love relate?
Our gross offenses, countless sins,
    Banished by blood that cleared our slate.

He paid the price for perfect peace,
    We sing His praises all the day;
Both God and man are satisfied,
    For blood has washed our sins away.

The stains are gone—oh, precious blood!
    Our conscience does not come between;
Nor filth nor fault of sin is here,
    For God has said that we are clean.

Now we are filled with such a peace;
    We will go on, and not in fear:
We will know peace that's sweeter still,
    And righteous at His side appear.

 

HYMN NO. 125

8.8.6.8.8.6. with repeat.

Thou Son of God, our Savior dear,
Through Thee redemption did appear,
    From sin and death to free.
Thy saints Thy blessings do expound,
Thou art with glory ever crowned,
    And seated heavenly.
    And seated heavenly.

Love of the Father lifted Thee,
Bestowed such power, majesty;
    Upon His right art Thou.
We praise Thy wisdom, Sovereign true,
And consecrate our all anew;
    Thou reignest ever now.
    Thou reignest ever now.

Head of the Church in righteousness,
Thy members Thou didst fully bless:
    All that Thou hast is ours.
Our life and power Thou become,
In all our motives, set Thy throne,
    And conquer Satan's pow'rs.
    And conquer Satan's pow'rs.

Not long that glor'ous day shall come,
Your Body will be raptured home,
    Ever to see Thy face.
Such happy day! Thyself to see,
And know that we belong to Thee,
    And rest in Thine embrace.
    And rest in Thine embrace.
