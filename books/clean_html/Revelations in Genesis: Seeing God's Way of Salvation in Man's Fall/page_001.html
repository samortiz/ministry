PREFACE

For three months, beginning from October 1954, Brother Witness Lee conducted a life training in which one of the classes considered the line of life in the whole Bible. The messages in the first part have been published in the book Revelations in Genesis: Seeing God’s Desire and Purpose in Creation. This book is a collection of edited records from the second part of that study of Genesis.
