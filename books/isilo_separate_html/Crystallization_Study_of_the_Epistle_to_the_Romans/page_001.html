PREFACE

This book is composed of messages given by Brother Witness Lee in Anaheim, California from August 24 through December 17, 1994, and from February 15 through April 19, 1995. These two series of messages were first published in two volumes under the titles The Crystallization of the Epistle to the Romans, Messages 1-17 and Messages 18-26, respectively.
