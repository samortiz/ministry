CHAPTER TWO

THE DIRECTION OF
THE LORD'S MOVE TODAY

Scripture Reading: 1 Tim. 3:15; Heb. 3:12; Matt. 16:18; Rev. 5:6b; Rom. 15:16; 1 Pet. 2:5, 9; Acts 5:42; John 21:15; Eph. 4:12; 1 Cor. 14:1, 3-5, 12, 23-26, 31; John 15:1, 5, 8a; Eph. 3:19; John 3:29-30; Rev. 19:7-8; 22:17; 21:1-2, 9b-10; Luke 19:12, 15a; Dan. 2:34-35, 44-45; Rev. 11:15

OUTLINE

  1. Our God is living—1 Tim. 3:15; Heb. 3:12.
  2. Our Lord is moving—Matt. 16:18.
  3. And the Spirit is working—Rev. 5:6b.
  4. What is the Lord's move today?
    1. To recover the New Testament priesthood of the gospel—Rom. 15:16; 1 Pet. 2:5, 9.
    2. To recover the home meetings for feeding the new believers—Acts 5:42; John 21:15.
    3. To recover the perfecting of the saints for the work of the New Testament ministry—Eph. 4:12.
    4. To recover prophesying for the building up of the church as the Body of Christ—1 Cor. 14:1, 3-5, 12, 23-26, 31.
  5. The direction of the Lord's move:
    1. To build up the organic Body of Christ as the organism of the processed and dispensing God in His divine Trinity for His full expression—Eph. 4:12; John 15:1, 5, 8a; Eph. 3:19.
    2. To prepare the bride as the counterpart of the Bridegroom for the eternal marriage of the redeeming God with His redeemed—John 3:29-30; Rev. 19:7-8; 22:17; 21:1-2, 9-10.
    3. To bring in the kingdom of God as the spreading of the divine life for God's eternal administration in the fulfillment of His eternal economy—Luke 19:12, 15a; Dan. 2:34-35, 44-45; Rev. 11:15.
