Eph 2:42
1 Father, to us Thy mercy Thou hast shown, Titus 3:5 1 Pet 1:3
In Thine own way;
Sinners like us as dear sons Thou wouldst own, 2 Cor 6:18
In love for aye.
Vessels of mercy, Thou didst us prepare; Rom 9:23
Vessels of honor, we Thyself declare. 2 Tim 2:20
2 "In mercy I delight, not sacrifice," Heb 10:6 Matt 9:132
Thou hast declared;
Thru this Thy mercy, favor in Thine eyes
We all have shared.
'Tis not of them that will nor them that run, Rom 9:16
But of Thee showing mercy as was done.
3 Father, Thy mercy with Thy love and grace 2 John 3 Eph 2:42
Did we obtain;
And in Thy mercy, with Thee face to face,
We'll e'er remain;
And for Thy mercy we would worship Thee Rom 15:9
Through all our days and through eternity.

Eph 2:4 But God, being rich in mercy, because of His great love with which He loved us,
fn 2 The object of love should be in a lovable condition, but the object of mercy is always in a pitiful situation. Hence, God's mercy reaches farther than His love. God loves us because we are the object of His selection. But because of our fall we became pitiful, even dead in our offenses and sins; therefore, we need God's mercy. Because of His great love, God is rich in mercy to save us out of our wretched position into a condition that is suitable for His love.
Titus 3:5 Not out of works in righteousness which we did but according to His mercy He saved us, through the washing of regeneration and the renewing of the Holy Spirit,
1 Pet 1:3 Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who according to His great mercy has regenerated us unto a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead,
2 Cor 6:18 And I will be a Father to you, and you will be sons and daughters to Me, says the Lord Almighty.
Rom 9:23 In order that He might make known the riches of His glory upon vessels of mercy, which He had before prepared unto glory,
2 Tim 2:20 But in a great house there are not only gold and silver vessels but also wooden and earthen; and some are unto honor, and some unto dishonor.
Heb 10:6 In burnt offerings and sacrifices for sin You did not delight.
Matt 9:13 But go and learn what this means, I desire mercy and not sacrifice, for I did not come to call the righteous, but sinners.
fn 2 Mercy is part of the grace that man receives from God. (See note 162 in Heb. 4.) But self-righteous men do not like to receive mercy or grace from God; they prefer to give something to God. This is contrary to God's way in His economy. Just as God desires to show mercy to pitiful sinners, so He wants us to show mercy in love to others (Micah 6:6-8; Mark 12:33).
Rom 9:16 So then it is not of him who wills, nor of him who runs, but of God who shows mercy.
2 John 3 Grace, mercy, peace will be with us from God the Father and from Jesus Christ, the Son of the Father, in truth and love.
