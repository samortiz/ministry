1 Life's too short
To waste a single day.
The kingdom life
Is closer every day.
In the midst of time,
The kingdom's mine
Just by calling on His name each day.
2 The sweetest taste
Of life is deep within.
Its flowing out
Is primed by calling Him.
In the midst of time,
The kingdom's mine.
Just by calling on His name each day.
3 Time is quickly
Running out each day,
The Lord will come
And swiftly end this age.
At the end of time,
The goal we see,
The Millennium is our entry.
At the end of time,
The goal we see,
The New Jerusalem we'll be.
