Introduction to Amos:
Author: Amos (1:1a).Time of His Ministry: About 787 B.C. (1:1b).Place of His Ministry: Bethel, in the northern kingdom of Israel (7:13).Object of His Ministry: The northern kingdom of Israel.

Subject of Amos:
Jehovah's Judgments on Israel and the Surrounding Nations,
with the Issue of Restoration
